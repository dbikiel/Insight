Ayappa's strongly wishes to travel by airplane. Kochavva helps the child to achieve this through his optimistic and practical ways. Ayappa learns what you love is important than what you wish in life.
This is a story of needs of children, innocence, selfishness, selflessness, winning attitude and definition of achievement narrated through Ayappa's, Ambili's and Kochavva's lives. The magic that happens when people can be modest and down to earth.
