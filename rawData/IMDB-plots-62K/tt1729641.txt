As boy was being born, all of Poland was busy staring on their television set, the Polish team was playing the game of the century, and Deyna was just leaving the field. The film is the story of boy, who was born in communist Poland, raised in the tumultuous times of the political transition and enters the new millennium with the family of his own. The day his wife gave birth to their baby daughter, all of Poland once again is watching the football game on television. Poland is beating Portugal. A coming-of-age film about love, and about life choices
