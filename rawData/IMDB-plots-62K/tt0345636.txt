After a comet passes the Earth it causes a cataclysmic storm. Only a disbanded government unit with a secret weather-manipulating weapon can try to stop it.
Guy (Dale Midkiff) loses wife in airplane incident during an experimental army mission in the eyewall of a hurricane. Meteor whips up super-storm and evil general (Michael Ironside) brings back same guy who finds himself - once again - on an airplane in even bigger hurricane. This time he must save the world from disaster...
