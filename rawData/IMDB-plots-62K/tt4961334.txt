Ullas will do anything to make money and never hesitates to cheat people. His life takes a turn when he decides to marry an Indian woman who is settled in Canada.
Ullas (Dileep) has a deep desire to earn money and to migrate to foreign countries.So he marries Laya (Mamta Mohandas) a Canada Settled Mallu Girl but failed to know that she was an alcoholic addict.The incidents faced by Ullas and Laya forms the rest of the story.
