When a young woman is cruelly and indiscriminately attacked by a notorious gang led by the violent Trey, her little 16 year old sister Kayla wants revenge and will stop at nothing to get it, even if it means joining a rival girl gang led by the volatile and damaged man-hating Danielle.
Kayla a rebellious teenage girl from Newcastle, ends up sleeping rough on the streets of London until she gains membership to a tough all-girl street gang and is then introduced to the ways of crime and extreme violence as a way of surviving.
Kayla, a young street naive rebellious teenage girl from Newcastle ends up sleeping rough in London eventually joining a hardened all-girl local street gang and turning to crime. Kayla needs help in finding a ruthless drugs dealer who indiscriminately killed her sister and is out to kill her too. Kayla is soon out of her depth and fighting for survival.
