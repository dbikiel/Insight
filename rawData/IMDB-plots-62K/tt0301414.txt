A bank robber arrives to do a job in a small French town and strikes up a friendship with a retired poetry teacher.
A teacher and a gangster meet by chance in a small town pharmacy. As a friendship of sorts develops between these opposite personalities, each starts to envy the other and by the week's end, everything will change for both of them.
Criminal Milan is sent to a small French town to knock off the local bank. With plans to make and no place to stay, he accepts a retired poetry teacher's offer of shelter. Quickly they find that their lives might have turned out better if they were in one another's shoes,.
