A close-knit trio navigates the idea of creating life, while at the same time being confronted with a brutal scenario.
Three friends trying to accomplish a life goal while working on crazy art proyect and struggling to find a place in the art business will depict a colourful, joyfull sample of Brooklyn's young adulthood life style in 2013. A duality of dreams and hopelessness. They will understand life can be a little nasty.
