A man wanders into the woods in search of his cat and witnesses a murder.
In this horror tale, a recluse, named Virgil, witnesses the murder of a young girl named Claire while searching the woods for his lost cat. When absolutely no evidence of the crime is found, the police stop believing Virgil's story. Haunted by nightmares of the girl's murder and by supernatural experiences, Virgil tries to piece together the bizarre mystery of Claire's life and death.
