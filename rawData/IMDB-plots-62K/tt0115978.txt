Various persons suspected of crimes and criminals speak and confess straight into the camera.
Documentary dated 1994 about crime in the US, presenting various persons suspected of crimes and criminals speak and confess straight into the camera. We meet small-time crooks, hookers, drug dealers, rapists and so on. The videotapes of the confessions and extract emerges the image of a common criminal: in total lack of feelings toward others - victims, relatives, friends. A slow motion personal compilation, that is "Cops"(TV series) opposite.
