Solovyov Sevastyanov G., a former clerk, suddenly decided to start a new life and a step on the path of Russian dashing bandit at the call of the blood of his ancestors ... Now Nightingale is a gang leader and ready to revolt - senseless and merciless! And while law enforcement agencies, intelligence agencies and even a secret agent, H7, are trying to close in on the gang of Nightingale, our hero, with his drive and mischief, restores justice in his native land and, in the process, won the love of ordinary voters. The robber is now for the people not a bandit but instead the ideal hero, the fearless warrior, to whom people dedicate rhyming songs. Never mind that sooner or later every "Tale" comes to an end ....
