A Secret Service Agent is held captive in the trunk of a car and endures mental and physical torture as terrorists attempt to extract information for their plot against the President of the United States.
What appears to be a random kidnapping becomes something more sinister when Secret Service Agent Jeremy Reins discovers he's being used as a pawn in a terrorist plot. Watching the clock tick down to an unknown catastrophe, Jeremy is forced by his captors to listen to the outside world on the brink of collapse, knowing that the only way to save the people he loves is to divulge a secret that he has sworn to protect.
Secret service agent, Jeremy awakens in a solid perspex box in the back of a car. He's told he has to give up a secret which he's sworn never divulge. If he doesn't, people he knows will die. While in the box he can hear and talk to others in the same situation via a C.B radio. At the same time there's a clock ticking down, making his situation more intense. A lot of lives depend on him and the secret he holds.
