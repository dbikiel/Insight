Two lonely girls plan to commit suicide and decide to chronicle their last 24 hours. Their last day takes them to the edge and back as they face past demons and the biggest decision of all...Do they go through with it?
Jade and Amber are two teenage girls who make a suicide pact with each other and decide to record their last 24 hours on film. Through flashbacks, it is revealed that Jade was infected with the HIV virus when she had sex for the first time, and Amber suffers emotional and physical abuse from her mother and her mother's boyfriend.
