An ambitious filmmaker plots a re-imagining of Hamlet set during the American Civil War, but when he loses his large budget he has to accept much less money--which means making major script changes.
Desperate filmmaker Osric Taylor dreams of making his civil war Hamlet but when production funding dries up he agrees to take southern matron Hester Beauchamps offer to fund the film if he let's her niece be in it. After Hester takes the wrong voodoo medicine, things take a turn for the worst.
