Tarzan comes to the rescue.
A beautiful, young girl becomes caught in Tarzan's net while he is fishing. She is Mara, who has fled from the pearl-fishing village of Aquatania to escape marriage to Palanth, who poses as the living-god, Balu. Balu's henchmen recapture Mara and return her to Balu's temple on Taboo Island. Tarzan follows in an effort to rescue her.
To escape from an arranged marriage to mythical Aquatania's pagan god, Balu, the desperate maiden, Mara, swims to the outside world, only to end up in Tarzan's fishing net. Without delay, Tarzan visits the idyllic secret paradise to right the wrongs--and before long--he finds himself before a wicked and well-planned conspiracy, as the island's high-priest, Palanth, is in league with the avaricious pearl trader, Varga, to rob the inhabitants of their precious treasure. Can Tarzan save the mermaid from the barbaric idol's will?
