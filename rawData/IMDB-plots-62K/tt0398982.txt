A down-on-his-luck divorced father struggles to get his life and family back together before it's too late.
A down-on-his-luck, divorced father works the night shift at an Atlantic City casino. When his relationship with his young daughters and ex-wife is jeopardized by a run-in with the law, he struggles to get his life - and family - back together before it's too late. A moving and humorous look at the limits of unconditional love and what defines a family.
Duane Hopwood. A father, sharing custody with his ex-wife Linda. One fatal night due to his drinking habits lead him to losing his kids whom he loves so much. It's pretty much downhill from there. Duane struggles to get his life back facing every battle he has ahead of him. including the greatest one of all, himself.
