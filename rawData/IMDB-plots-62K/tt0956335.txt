On a new TV reality series titled "Survival Island 2020" contestants are deposited on a remote island, all of whom are felons facing long prison sentences who compete for their freedom and a $50 million jackpot. But little do they know that the island is a home for a bloodthirsty horde of flesh-eating zombies.
In the future death row criminals are given a chance to pardon them selfs on a "survivor" style reality show! Only to find the island is full of zombie ex-contestants!
