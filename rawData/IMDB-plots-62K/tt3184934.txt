A young woman makes a surprising discovery about the husband of her late best friend.
Claire and Laura have been friends since primary school. Now, in their adult lives, with each happily married, to Gilles and David respectively, they are as close as ever. Then Laura dies after an illness and Claire's world is suddenly very empty. Being the godmother of Laura's child, Lucie, Claire visits David and Lucie, only to discover he has a startling secret...
Utterly inseparable, the childhood best friends, Claire and Laura, share everything. Happily married to Gilles and David, the lifelong companions seem to live the perfect life; however, Claire's world will turn upside down, when Laura, unexpectedly, becomes ill and dies. Now, Laura's devastated husband, David, is burdened with the difficult task of being both a father and a mother to his newborn orphaned child, Lucie, as an equally grief-stricken Claire struggles to come to terms with the unfair tragedy. Then, Claire decides to check in on David and her little god-daughter, only to come face-to-face with an unsettling sight, and a shocking revelation. Undoubtedly, David has been harbouring a bizarre secret. Can Claire handle the truth?
