Betrayed and on the run, a North Korean agent tries to uncover the volatile secrets hidden inside the eyeglasses of a dead man.
Dong-chul (Gong Yoo) is the best field agent in North Korea - until he is abandoned during a mission, his wife and daughter murdered. Hunted and on the run, torn between grief and vengeance, he takes a job as a night driver for the CEO of a powerful corporation. The chairman is brutally assassinated - but gives Dong-chul a pair of glasses before he dies. Now, he's on the run again. Accused of murder, wanted for treason, and desperate to uncover the volatile national secrets hidden inside the glasses. Dong-Chul wants the truth. And he'll start a war to get it.
Abandoned by his government and with nearly every spy after him, the once North Korean lethal super-spy, Ji Dong-chul, defects to the South convinced that his wife and little daughter are dead. Framed for the murder of a millionaire CEO, Dong-chul will flee to Seoul with a pair of glasses of the utmost importance to both sides, as the embittered government agent, Colonel Min Se-hoon, commences a relentless manhunt. Can the wronged operative unearth the truth behind the mysterious assassination, or will he share his family's fate?
