After girlfriend Laura dumps him for another man, Bruno plots to come between the new lovers. But his plan takes an unexpected turn, causing Bruno to question his own sexuality.
Bruno is dumped by his girlfriend; behind a calm, indifferent expression, his mind plans a cold, sweet vengeance. She, a modern girl, keeps on seeing him once in a while, but has another boyfriend, Pablo. Bruno becomes Pablo's friend, with the idea of eroding the couple, maybe introducing him to another woman. But, along the way, the possibility of a plan B arises, a more effective one, which will put his own sexuality into question.
After Laura (Mercedes Quinteros), his girlfriend, breaks up with Bruno (Manuel Vignau), she starts to date Pablo (Lucas Ferraro). Bruno hatches a plan to revenge himself by trying to break the new couple up. His plan involves becoming friends with Pablo, but the plan soon veers off track. While building his friendship with Pablo, and hooking up with Laura now and again, Bruno begins to question his own sexuality. First time Argentine director Marco Berger brings us a modern, touching urban tale.
