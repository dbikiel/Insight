Two Japanese scientists, Ushioda and Ochi, develop a bond with their sled dogs while on an expedition in Antarctica. Ushioda and Ochi eventually leave Antarctica, only to return to search for the dogs inadvertently marooned there.
A group of scientists leave for Antarctica with sleigh dogs. In order to save their lives, they have to leave Antarctica a hurry, abandoning their dogs behind. We follow the dogs for the rest of the movie, as they are fighting for their survival in the coolest place on earth.
