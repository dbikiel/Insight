Raju, a waiter, is in love with the famous TV reporter Greeta Kapoor. After a man is murdered, Kapoor shows up at Raju's door to ask him some questions - it turns out that Raju served the dead man his last supper, and the authorities hope that he might be able to help them. Raju lies and says that he was an eye witness, in order to spend more time with Kapoor. He gives the police a false description of the killer, but it matches his best friend Kutti, so soon Kutti is wanted by the police, and the Mafia, who is responsible for the killing, is after Raju.
A waiter by profession; Rajiv Bhatnagar has romantic feelings for TV reporter Geeta. Finding an opportunity to be close to Geeta; Rajiv decides to pretend he is a witness in a murder case and agrees to testify on live television on the same channel that Geeta works for. He does give the description of a man which is indeed the killer putting both he and his lover's lives in jeopardy.
Employed as a mere waiter in Sanman Restaurant, Rajiv Bhatnagar dreams of news-reporter, Geeta Kapoor. Imagine his delight when she approaches him to find out if he was a witness in a recent murder. Rajiv decides to lie in order to be close to Geeta, and agrees to be publicly interviewed on national television. During this interview, he unwittingly admits that he saw the killer, and describes a man, he saw in the elevator. As it turns out, this man was indeed the real killer, and now Rajiv and Geeta must run for their lives themselves as the real killers are after them.
