Chloe, a young woman, is going on holidays. She entrusts her beloved cat to Madame Renée's care. But one day Madame Renée (an old lady of the neighborhood) can not find the cat. Chloe starts searching the neighborhood... This is the pretext for the exploration of a quarter of Paris and his inhabitants.
In Paris, the lonely assistant makeup artist Chloe shares her apartment with a homosexual. When she needs to travel, she asks him to take care of her beloved cat Gris-Gris but he refuses. Chloe seeks out someone to take care of her cat and she meets Madame Renée that offers to take care of Gris-Gris with her cats. When she returns, Madame Renée explains that Gris-Gris has fled. Chloe searches the neighborhood and finds solidarity with her neighbors.
