Antoine, a daydreaming student, is woke up by the sound of the voice of his landlord which recalls him to empty his flat before 1 PM. For Antoine it's almost impossible. His one room flat is a total mess. Only one solution to be on time and not to lose the 3 months deposit: bring all of his pals. To solve this problem Antoine will faces a maze of unimaginable complications and discover that often the heavier in a move out, are mates.
