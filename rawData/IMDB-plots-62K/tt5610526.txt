In the middle of a desert, soldiers are protecting a marvelous pearl, but a huge creature is going to steal it.
In a mysterious and barren land bounded by an inhospitable and frightful desert, an impregnable stronghold stands tall. Atop its proudest tower, a matchless iridescent pearl shines and an entire undaunted garrison is assembled to protect it. But many desire the castle's most precious treasure, and this time, an unstoppable adversary is at the gates. Who shall prevail?
