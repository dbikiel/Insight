Based on 1988 book by Luanne Rice.
Three generations of women live on a small island off the coast of Washington state with their men with whom they have no end of problems. While making a documentary of martial violence Georgie finds herself giving into the overwhelming jealous urges that put a strain on her marriage. Through revelations about her parents and sister's relationships and a flirtation of her own Georgie comes to grips with live on and off the island.
The grandmother has been a widow since 1931 when she was younger than her granddaughter Georgie who has spent her entire life on an island near Seattle with grandmother, mother and her sister Clare. Living in an emotional island as well, Georgie makes a foray into the outside world and learns a lot about herself and three generations of women who have had a very hard time indeed keeping men in their lives.
