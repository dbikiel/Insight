Former Danish servicemen Lars and Jimmy are thrown together while training in a neo-Nazi group. Moving from hostility through grudging admiration to friendship and finally passion, events take a darker turn when their illicit relationship is uncovered.
Broderskab/ Brotherhood, er en historie om kærlighed og identitet. 22-årige Lars bliver tiltrukket af det nynazistiske miljø, og ikke mindst af Jimmy. Der udvikler sig et kærlighedsforhold mellem de to, og den slags sker ikke ustraffet i de kredse. Men kærligheden og den erotiske tiltrækning er så stærk, at de på trods af alle nynazistiske regler fortsætter deres eventyrBrotherhood.
A a story about love and identity. Lars, 22, is drawn to the neo-Nazi scene, and to Jimmy. A love affair grows between them. Though gay love is not something to go unpunished in their circles, their love and erotic attraction are so strong that they continue their affair, even in the face of their fellow neo-Nazis' condemnation.
