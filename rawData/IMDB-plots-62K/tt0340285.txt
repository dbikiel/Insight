After the passing of his wife, a man learns he is infirtile and their son cannot be his.
Armin is in crisis. One fateful day he discovers he is infertile and has been throughout his life. From this he discovers his first wife, Monika was unfaithful before she died, how else could she have had the child that Armin thought was always his. His paranoia drives him to find the truth and in turn pushes those closest to him further away, particularly his now teenage son, Bo. But Armin is unprepared just how deep those closest to him are involved.Armin wants a child with his new girlfriend, though he already has a 13-year-old son Bo, but that stems from his previous relationship with his deceased girlfriend Monika. After investigating shows that Armin is infertile and that has always been so. Armin is stunned and takes his son and girlfriend Ellen into his emotions. Now he knows that Bo is not his son, he goes in search of the biological father, the story gets a huge turn.Armin suspect any man who has been in Monika's life, and he must and will get the answers. In the end it appears that Armin's father is also the father of Bo...
