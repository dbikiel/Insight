Rambabu (Venkatesh Daggubati), a cable operator, tries to protect his family from paying the price for their accidental crime.
Rambabu(Venkatesh) is a cable operator and a movie-buff, living merrily in a small village near Vijayanagaram, with his understanding wife Jyothi(Meena) and two children(girls). When the family accidentally involve in an event that can become a serious problem, Rambabu with his picture-smart brain tries to save the family from the consequences of it. But, can they outsmart the police and getaway with it?
