The conductor goes with the orchestra to Jerusalem to execute the oratorio "Passion according to St.Matthew". But tours are wrapped in the tragedy. The black sun of Jerusalem unmasks with main the hero of the successful artist, forces to see itself in ruthless nakedness of egoism and cruelty. Sometimes in 3 days of people can overestimate and rethink the life completely. To change never late.
The famous conductor Petrov is prepared to travel with his Orchestra to Jerusalem, to give a concert. Eve receives a telegram informing him of the suicide of his son, who hitherto lived in that city, and which at the time denied by his Bohemian life and its relationship with drugs. Members of the Orchestra have their own problems: jealousy, insecurity...
