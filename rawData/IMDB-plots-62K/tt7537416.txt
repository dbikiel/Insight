Two teams, one ball - a starting whistle.
A young athlete comes to the stadium, where already takes place a football match between two popular teams "Astra" and "Tulip". In the match, goal after the goal is "stuffed" one of the playing teams - "Astra" literally "destroys" the "Tulip". With the score 4-0, "Tulip" on the counterattack is given to score the so-called "goal of prestige" - after which the ball after the ball again flies into the long-suffering gate of "Tulip" - the game ends with a score of 10:1. Under the strongest impression, the young athlete offers to train "Tulip ".
