All all-female gang of Old West bank robbers comes out of retirement to rescue a sister in peril.
Out to avenge her sister's death at the hands of a female gang member "Little Suzie" (Charity HILL), Rachel (Monica CALHOUN) calls together her friends; Kim (Stacey DASH), Ming Li (Marie MATIKO), Chastity (Lil' Kim), and Maria (LisaRaye); who formerly made up the infamous Rose Gang. Rachel's friends, lacking Rachels' motivation for revenge, are hesitant to undertake this dangerous task. Rachel, having lived in the town which is controlled by Suzie's gang, reveals to her friends that the town hides two halves of a secret map, that when put together shows the location of hidden treasure. With the possibility of treasure strengthening their bond, the Rose Gang seeks out Little Suzie, the whereabouts of the maps and possibility of the riches revealed by them.
