A Uruguayan rugby team stranded in the snow swept Andes are forced to use desperate measures to survive after a plane crash.
In 1972, the Uruguayan rugby team is flying to Chile to play a game. However, the plane from the Uruguayan Air Force with 45 people crashes on the Andes Mountains and after the search party, they are considered dead. Two months after the crash, the sixteen survivors are finally rescued. Along the days, the starved survivors decide to eat flesh from the bodies of their comrades to survive.
When a rugby team's plane to Chile crashes in the Andes Mountains,they will do anything to survive,even if that means they have to eat their loved ones who are dead as food.Nando,Reberto,and Tintin leave the plane to find the tail to work the radio.Once they brought it back,it didn't work.So they went out again but Tintin stayed back or went back.Nando and Reberto find the green vallies of Chile and finally breech the Andes and found help.The obstacles they faced were avalanches,food,shelter,and a way to get out.They acused the pilots of being drunk and it was their fault.The good thing is that they escaped.
In 1972, a rugby team from South America goes to Chile to play a game. As they approach, they crash in the Andes mountains and struggle to survive. They have to go through eating their loved ones, avalanches, and finding the tail to the plane to get the transmitter since the batteries are in the tail. And they also meet this guy who has mental issues. Will they make it out alive?
The amazing, true story of a Uruguayan rugby team's plane that crashed in the middle of the Andes mountains, and their immense will to survive and pull through alive, forced to do anything and everything they could to stay alive on meager rations and through the freezing cold. The only thing the team has riding on after losing so many of their good friends and family members is the slim chance of making it through alive and their faithfulness to God.
