José is a football agent. He is in Ivory Coast to spot young talents in football . José Brussels is a braggart , who willingly sleeps with Gigi , a beautiful African woman who loves above all the luxury he offers her ... One day, José spots Yaya, a street boy with the potential of a champion , and brings him to Belgium . Will Yaya be José's reward? Between the poor neighborhoods of Abidjan and Sporting Club of Charleroi, the cultural "gap" is brutal ... and reserves surprises .
José (Belgian, 45 years) is a manager of African football and he has chosen the poor neighborhoods of Abidjan as a hunting ground. There, women sell their charm while the guys, addicted to football, have the mad dream of conquering Europe and his astronomical wages.
In this context, José finds Yaya, a young prodigy. He promises him a great future in football. He picks him in his bags. But, in Belgium, nothing goes as planned. The day of his test in a great club, Yaya makes a malaria crisis. Finally, the goose that lays the golden egg becomes a bulky object for his manager. However, by his rebellious and direct character, Yaya gonna open the soul of his employer.
