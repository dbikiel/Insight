Gautham's life takes some unexpected turns after realizing that "the greatest mystery in the world is human life itself." What unfolds is a maze of both the trivial and the sublime.
A group of people who are friends from their campus days. They try to estrange Gautham from his friends as they believe that this friendship spoils his studies and future life. But later after their college life all of them have a reunion except Sreya who is in Goa.
