Celal, who dreams of achieving happiness as a ladies' man and charmer, soon gets into difficulties and suffers misfortune. Back when Celal was in a relationship, he was jealous of the life of a bachelor. Now he has to face the bitter recognition that this isn't what he wanted and pulls out all the stops to try to get his Ceren back. None of the women he meets have what Ceren had. But he doesn't appreciate her value until she is gone and then pursues her relentlessly. But it's all too late for Ceren. Her heart is broken and won't mend. But Celal refuses to give up, because "everyone has a Ceren".
