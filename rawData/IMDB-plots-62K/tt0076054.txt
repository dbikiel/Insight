A mother and daughter find their personalities switched and have to live each other's lives on one strange Friday.
Schoolgirl Annabel is hassled by her mother, and Mrs. Andrews is annoyed with her daughter, Annabel. They each think that the other has an easy life. On a normal Friday morning, each complain about the other and wish she could have the easy life of her daughter/mother for just one day and their wishes come true as a bit of magic puts Annabel in Mrs. Andrews' body and vice versa. They each have a Freaky Friday.
The teenager Annabel has friction with her mother, Mrs. Andrews and her younger brother, Ben. One Friday morning, they each wish to be the other for one day at the same time and their wishes come true and they swap bodies. Meanwhike, Mr. Andrews, who is the PR of a company, is preparing a party to promote a real estate deal and he counts on the support of his wife and daughter. During the day, Annabel and Mrs. Andrews learn the difficulties of each other and understand the difficulties.
