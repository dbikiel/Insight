A put-upon teen finds himself reliving the same miserable Christmas day over and over again.
An overlooked middle child finds himself in the unexpected spotlight when he realizes his family's terrible Christmas day keeps repeating. As the only one experiencing the day over and over, he decides to use his unique gift to give the holidays a makeover and his family a Christmas they will never forget.
Life is tough for Pete as he lives in the shadow of his football fanatic older brother and perfect,intelligent younger brother. His dad has recently lost his job and finds himself constantly at loggerheads with Pete's grandad. Christmas Day has been an absolute disaster and Pete has to relive it over and over again.
