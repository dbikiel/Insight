Twardowsky is heading to the border of the Solar System. Can he make it?. The Devil, and he's hunter - Lucy are right behind him. Can desperate Polish astronaut win with his fiendish opponents?
Twardowsky is heading to the border of the Solar System. After defeating Lucy (she-devil send to collect his soul) on the moon, he's running out of gas somewhere around Saturn. Devil send Lucy after Twardowsky again, to end what started on the moon. Will cunning Polish astronaut find a way from this mess and keep his precious soul?
