A pair of working-class brothers flee their Reno Motel after getting involved in a fatal hit-and-run accident.
Two brothers who are forced to leave Reno after being involved in a hit-and-run accident. Based on the novel by Willy Vlautin, this moody thriller is a searing and profound examination of brotherhood set in the timeless Sierra Nevadan frontier.
This award-winning festival hit stars Emile Hirsch ("Into the Wild") and Stephen Dorff ("Somewhere") as two brothers who are forced to flee Reno after being involved in a hit-and-run accident. Based on the novel by Willy Vlautin, this moody thriller is a searing and profound examination of brotherhood and the power of storytelling set in the timeless Sierra Nevadan frontier. Features supporting performances by Dakota Fanning ("The Twilight Saga") and Kris Kristofferson ("Lone Star").
