No treason, no surrender.
Three daughters battle for acceptance, redemption and new opportunities. ********Teenager Dora is a neglected girl who dreams about becoming a football manager. She finds a role model in Zdravko Mamic, the controversial manager of the most successful Croatian football club, Dinamo Zagreb. She spends her days on the Internet studying him and exploring content which is inappropriate for a child of her age: corruption, trials and constant clashes in the football world. She lives with her mother, Lidija and her brother Jan. One day, after four years of absence her outlaw father Vedran reappears. Her mother is deeply unhappy. She can't stand either her estranged husband, or her own children. She instinctively saves her father when the police knock on their door with a search warrant. She finds what she's been missing all these years in him: parental love, attention and care. She resolves to do all everything she can to keep him in her life. *******Ives has no rest because her mornings start with the care of her father, who has Alzheimer's. Her life is filled with many small obligations that exhaust her, and plans for a nicer life are, increasingly, out of reach. As she loses him, she gradually loses the ground beneath her feet. *******Natasa, successful TV producer, is pregnant. Her husband, Marin cheats on her with Lidija, Dora's mother. When things go awry for her, she decides to confront her estranged father, Slavko. The ominous misgivings about the state of health are confirmed and her confrontation with an uncertain future begins. All three carry their fathers, yet they need them to confront themselves.
