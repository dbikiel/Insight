Although Alexia, Franco's ex-girlfriend, has been deceased for some time, Franco still has her as a contact on his social network. When he decides to delete her and move on, something strange starts to manifest through his computer.
Franco's ex-girlfriend Alexia had committed suicide some time ago, but he still feels her presence because her social network profile has not yet been taken down. On the day of her birthday, he visits her page and feels guilty over the fact she had taken her own life. When Franco's new girlfriend finally convinces him to delete Alexia from his contacts and move on with his life, his computer starts to malfunction and some thing strange and sinister manifests through the web and into his life. Maybe a CLICK isn't enough to leave the past behind.
Depressed and slowly starting to come to terms with the terrible loss of his late ex-girlfriend, Alexia, Franco takes the decision to delete her contact from his social network, struggling to make a fresh start with a new companion. However, as strange manifestations begin taking place in Franco's computer, it will become evident that some loves really are forever.
