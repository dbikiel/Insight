Four Jewish intellectuals carpool to the funeral of their old friend Leslie Braverman, who died suddenly at age 41.
Friends gather for the funeral of a friend. Sub-plots unfold as the group attempts to link-up to attend the funeral of their friend, Braverman.
Four Jewish intellectuals and writers are shocked to hear that their friend Leslie Braverman has died suddenly from a heart attack. They decide to carpool it in a crowded Volkswagon Beetle from Manhattan to Brooklyn to attend the funeral, but they become lost and their odyssey becomes a journey of self-discovery.
