Right when everything is going well for Erwin, his father falls sick and asks him to drop everything and help out at the family store disappointing Yohan, his irresponsible older brother.
Cek Toko Sebelah is about the tale of a widowed father, left with his shop to take care of. When he gets a heart attack, he leaves his shop to the hands of his youngest son, Erwin. This is an epic movie that tells about the story of how the shop gets sold, and Erwin and his brother, Yudi, devise a plot to win the shop back.
