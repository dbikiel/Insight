Investigators pursuing a bizarre hemorrhagic illness are lead to a strange black painting that they discover is a portal to another dimension.
April Meddows, is a medical investigator, working for the state communicable disease unit CDC. Her current investigation is in what at first is believed to be a very dangerous case of raging and gory hemorrhagic fever assumed to be E-bola. But when the dead man turns out to have no signs of any illness in any of their tests, the mystery deepens.
