Arcachon. August. Jean-Pierre Savelli, an employee at the Mutuelles d'Assurances in Clermont-Ferrand, learns that Valérie, his fiancée, wants to take a break. To recharge his batteries and find calm and serenity, he decides to change his holiday destination - He lands at the Camping des Flots Bleus and falls on Patrick Chirac and his band of irreducible campers. The holidays can begin . Aperitif.
