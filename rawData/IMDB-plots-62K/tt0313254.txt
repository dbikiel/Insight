Rock sensation Barbie and her band The Rockers, beloved by everyone all around the world, decide to do something no band has ever done before - preform a charity rock concert in outer space.
After completing a sensational world tour, Barbie, the lead singer of the Rockers--the most famous band on Earth--has already something in mind for their next live performance. As the first ambassador for world peace, the fashionable songstress boldly decides to take things to the next level, and perform where no other band has dared to go: the vast outer space. Now, Ken, Dee Dee, Dana, Diva, and Derek can't wait to rock the stars at the galactic Inter-star Amphitheatre. Are you ready for an out-of-this-world gig?
