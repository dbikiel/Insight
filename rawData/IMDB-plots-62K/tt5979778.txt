Asher, a vampiric necromancer, saves a girl being targeted by three cannibalistic witches. The two team up to kill the degenerates not knowing that the witches have chosen Asher as their next target.
Asher, a vampiric necromancer has settled in the small town of Shadow Grove, thinking that he can take a break from defeating evil forces. But his plans quickly change when he saves a girl in the woods named Lily before she can be killed by Rose, one of three cannibalistic witches. The two team up to defeat them before they can kill Lily. But the witches have now made Asher their prime target and begin to close in on the two. As a romance blossoms between Asher and Lily, they begin to realize that defeating that witches will be harder than they expected. But before they can prepare for the massacre, Asher is kidnapped and is taken to their dungeon to become their ultimate meal. But Asher has a few tricks up his sleeve and he'll make sure that this isn't the end, but the only the beginning. A dark tale of those who exist in the twilight realm where witchcraft and the creatures beyond are very real and very dangerous.
