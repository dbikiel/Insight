A simple yet timeless love story between a man and a woman, told using scenes edited together from hundreds of other films.
A film where anything can happen - the hero and the heroine changes their faces, age, look, names, and so on. The only same thing: the LOVE between man and woman... in an archetypical love story cut from 500 classics from all around the world
The story of this film is sweet and simple. On a perfectly normal day, after waking up, showering, and walking out of his house, a man bumps into a woman. It is love at first sight. He follows her to where she works as a singer, sneaks backstage to meet her, and they have the most perfect date - a fairground, dinner, and he stays the night. After fighting off the previous man in her life, he asks her to marry him. They get married, and go on honeymoon. Time passes, and real life comes along. Can they keep their love strong?The genius of this film is that it is all done by taking short clips of hundreds and hundreds of other famous films.
