Sergio Konstanza, a Swindler, owes money to Mr. Hasson. He escapes to the desert where he joins a wacky army reserve unit whose Sergeant is engaged to one of Mr. Hasson's Daughters.
Sergeant Moked wants to marry Yaeli, but her tough father Mr. Hasson refuses until her older sister is married first. Moked also has to recruit to reserves a sleek con man of Rumanian origin, Sergio Konstanza. In addition, Konstanza owes Hasson a lot of money after selling him a piece of land from the Mediterranean Sea. When Moked and Konstanza head to the military base in Sinai, Yaeli joins as a stowaway while Hasson, who wants his money back, goes after them.
