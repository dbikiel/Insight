Two best friends, Rob and GW, spend a night out drinking before Rob moves away to start a new business.
Two best friends spend a night out partying and drinking the before one is leaving their home town to start a business elsewhere. GW is preoccupied by his University finals and the crazy boss at his pet-store job, Mr. Layhie. Meanwhile, Rob has some big news to tell GW--aside from the plans for his new marijuana business. He is nervous about it, so they go drinking at a number of establishments before he gets the courage to tell him.
