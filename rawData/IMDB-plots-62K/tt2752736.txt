A long time ago, in the summer of 1981, a tragedy of epic proportions sends five friends on an epic adventure. All they have to do is follow The Stream and back. What could possibly go wrong? Encountering bullies, a nasty storm and mystical creatures, the friends will need the force of friendship to prevail. Coming-of-age comedy features Rainn Wilson, Mario Lopez, Kelly Rutherford and Christopher Gorham. Majority of proceeds from film benefit Boys &amp; Girls Clubs of America.
