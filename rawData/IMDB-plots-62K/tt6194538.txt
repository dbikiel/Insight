Little Spirou, like all his family before him, has a career destiny all traced. When his mother announces that he will integrate the grooms school at the beginning of the next school year, Petit Spirou, with the help of his friends, will take advantage of his last days of class to declare his love to Suzette.
Spirou Junior is destined for the family tradition, working in a hotel. So his mother transfers him from regular school to a private hotel staff course and his retired grandpa personally grooms him for the starting stage, as bellboy. However Spirou is determined to win over his present classmate Suzette, by inviting her on a 'world tour'. He recruits all his schoolmates to make unlikely preparations for an atypical travel adventure, suitable to get to now Suzette better.
