A look at the 2013 and 2014 civil unrest in the Ukrainian capital's central square.
A chronicle of the civil uprising against the regime of president Yanukovych that took place in Kiev (Ukraine) in the winter of 2013/14. The film follows the progress of the revolution: from peaceful rallies, half a million strong, in the Maidan square, to the bloody street battles between protesters and riot police.
A documentary presented as a 'live' and dramatic historical scene. University launched a revolution which as been relayed by mass-media. The pacific students' ideas for a new Ukrainia are step by step amplified and corrupted by other participants. Bad working conditions from a large range of population are revelated. Oligarchy is described dramatically as the origin of an increasing violence.
