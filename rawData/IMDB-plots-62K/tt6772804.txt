After security expert Ray Breslin is hired to rescue the kidnapped daughter of a Hong Kong tech mogul from a formidable Latvian prison, Breslin's girlfriend is also captured.
The daughter of a Hong Kong tech executive goes missing in what appears to be a routine ransom job. As Breslin and his crew delve deeper, they discover the culprit is the deranged son of one of their former foes, who also kidnapped Breslin's love and is holding her inside the massive prison known as Devil's Station.
After escaping death by the skin of his teeth in Escape Plan 2: Hades (2018), the Houdini of breakouts, Ray Breslin, finds himself in the impenetrable Latvian prison known as the "Devil's Station", to retrieve Daya, the abducted daughter of a powerful Hong Kong tech mogul. Targeted by the son of an old enemy, Ray and his team--Hush, Trent DeRosa, and the businessman's bodyguard, Bao--must infiltrate the fortified compound, before the determined kidnapper starts killing the hostages. However, this time, it's personal, and Ray has one more reason to succeed at all costs. Can the Extractors make it happen?
