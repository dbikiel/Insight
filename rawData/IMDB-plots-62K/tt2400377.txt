Uma loses her husband at a very young age and faces many hardships. The only support she gets is from her brother-in-law.
Hari lives with his wife Tara younger brother Mahadav and widowed aunt Namo Aatya.Mahdav's marriage is arranged to a very young Uma.Mahadav dies soon after their marriage.The villagers now want Uma to follow their traditions of shaving her head and living her entire life as Mahadav's widow. Hari opposes to all barriers put against Uma and stands by her in everything.Which raises doubt about their relationship.
