In the empty house of his family, Ramon, a poet, remembers the last day of the life of his master: the last time he went out with his friend don Latino de Hispalis, his talk with a minister and his dinner with Ruben Dario.
Tells the story of the last night of Max Star an old blind poet, who barely manages to keep his wife and daughter, and Don Latino, Max's best friend. We can also see the poet Ruben Dario and many other characters and prominent names of the history of Spain. Write By Griel Dx17.
