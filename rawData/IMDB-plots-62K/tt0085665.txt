What starts off as a squadron of American daredevil bombers in World War II, branded by society as renegades, turns into a revealing ride into the world of honor, violence, and undying passion for motorcycles on the road.
In 1930 Director Howard Hughes epic movie Hell's Angels starring Jean Harlow and Ben Lyons premiered on 47th street in New York City, about World War 1 aviator's. In World War 2, Captain Ira Baldwin and crew took the name from Hughes movie , and called their bomber squadron Hell's Angels. After the war the Hells Angels Motorcycle Club started in Fontana, California March 17 1948. (HELLS ANGELS BERDOO)
