PURASANGRE tells the story of how a crew of 5 professionals are forced to rob a famed Horse Racetrack and Casino complex. The plan is simple; stash the loot for five years and stay under the radar. But just a few months in, one of them resurfaces on the police radar, and his mistakes will result in the deadliest manhunt in history, spanning over 5 years to apprehend the other four remaining members, and recover the money.
