An ambitious software engineer employs a quirky girl to act as his girlfriend to avoid an arranged marriage. He starts to lose his ambitions as he gets close to her.
An ambitious software engineer, Karthik, has a dream of climbing up the corporate ladder and get a high paying job in USA. He stays away from family, sacrifices his social life and personal interests for his ambition. He employs a short film actress to act as his girlfriend, Siri, to avoid an arranged marriage. The quirky girl is more than he bargained for and impresses his father. Karthik too falls in love with her but as soon as he notices that he is drifting from his professional goals, he decides to avoid her. But when she returns to his life, he introspects his personal and professional choices in life.
