The intertwining lives of three men reveal that each deal with his problems in different, self-destructive ways
This powerful drama starring Sharon Stone, Timothy Hutton, Dylan Baker and Pruitt Taylor Vince embraces the lives of four unlikely heroes each consumed in a personal struggle to make sense of their lives and transforms them in ways they never imagined.
When a Man Falls in the Forest is a deeply moving apologue about the passions that drive us, the disappointments that challenge us and is a timely tribute to those whose sacrifice reinvigorates our lives and permits us to touch the people that matter most to us.
