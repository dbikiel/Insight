A young woman, dealing with anorexia, meets an unconventional doctor who challenges her to face her condition and embrace life.
Ellen is a 20 year old woman struggling with anorexia nervosa. In the midst of family problems and her own fears, she is accepted into a group home run by an unusual doctor. Through the people she meets and the journey she takes, Ellen follows a path of self discovery and acceptance that will lead her to a surprising place she never would have thought possible
Ellen or Eli is a 20 year old woman suffering from anorexia nervosa. When her step mother &amp; absent father run out of options, they manage to get an appointment with a very successful but unorthodox doctor. When Ellie accepts the help she's placed into a group home with other people suffering from eating disorders. Because she's with people just like her, Ellie finds herself facing the truth of her disease. Will she find herself before its too late or will death come knocking faster than expected?
