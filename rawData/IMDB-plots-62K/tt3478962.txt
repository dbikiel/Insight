Tony is admitted to a rehabilitation center after a serious ski accident. Dependent on the medical staff and pain relievers, she takes time to look back on a turbulent relationship that she experienced with Georgio. Why did they love each other? Who is this man that she loved so deeply? How did she allow herself to submit to this suffocating and destructive passion? For Tony, a difficult process of healing is in front of her, physical work which may finally set her free.
The ups and downs of a long-term relationship is explored and analyzed in a series of flashbacks. While at rehab after suffering a skiing accident, Toni recollects memories from her most important and also most difficult relationship with a man, the hopeless restaurateur Georgio, and how things got from a nice partnership to something wrong and complicated.
Tony is a charming woman, who at one of the parties met a handsome man Giorgio. She liked him at first sight, and she also interested him. Gradually an easy hobby grew into a crazy passion, and they simply could not imagine life without each other. For ten years they experienced all-consuming love, full of boundless happiness and blinding jealousy. But once Tony realized that she could no longer endure this painful love, and decided that it was time for them to break up.
