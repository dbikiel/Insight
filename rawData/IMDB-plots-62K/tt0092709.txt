Tito and Mary meet at the airport, where he gives her his extra ticket not being aware of the fatal situation in which he will fall as a result of his kindness.
Traveling from New York to Italy, pretty Mary runs into a moody, macho lawyer Tito. The girl immediately falls for him with the gruff manners and in an impertinent ways, she leans on him as she gets in trouble: Not only that she gets tied up in her suite by some gangsters, but also, her new husband is found dead.
