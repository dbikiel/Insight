This boisterous comedy, set against the backdrop of college life, proves that every fantasy has a catch.
Alvin is the shy, geeky looking nerd on campus. The only sex he ever has is with his imaginary girlfriend. But one day, he falls in love with a live girl, Sue. He starts daydreaming about her and soon realizes that she also is a dreamer. Only, when they dream, the other person can feel it too...
