An orthodox youngster and a free-spirited lady fall in love, only to understand that they are different in all aspects of life. How do they realise their complex requirements and mistakes?
An independent straight forward woman, comes across an surprisingly honest guy in her life, the circumstances life puts them in and how they deal with it, forms the crux of the story. Portrays the city's developing modern culture and the true colors of the different characters in raw fashion.
