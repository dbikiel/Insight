The movie about events of October revolution of 1917 and Lenin's role in the organization of revolt of Bolsheviks.
The events of the October Revolution of 1917 and the role of Lenin in organizing the rebellion of the Bolsheviks. In Petrograd, unrest, among the workers unrest. Lenin secretly arrives at the Petrograd station, through the cordon he is guided by a bodyguard - the worker Vasiliy. Lenin meets with Stalin, who is shown as his closest ally. Soon, an underground meeting of the Central Committee of the Bolshevik Party begins, where an uprising is being prepared.
