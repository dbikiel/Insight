An older beetle of a rare variety advises a young passerby against crossing a stream, where he is likely to get trapped by bug collector Donald, as he himself almost was when he was young.
An old Bootle beetle, a rare bread crazed by entomologists, tells to a youngster how he started his adventure as a youth leaving the forest across the river. Suddenly he faced a towering monster: Donald Duck, who smelled money for such a rare insect specimen and caught him in a jar, but curiously took a look and thus allowed the beetle to escape; the greedy duck ...
