A young man spurs romance and helps his friend and himself go through times and struggles of their ordinary life in Denmark.
Daniel is an irresponsible but nonetheless charming loser that makes a living by spraying other peoples love declarations onto walls.With a net income of 7 krones in the last four years he starts to be suspicious to the fiscal authoroties. Also the rest of his life he seems to fail because of his carelessness, may it be with the police or his landlord. he never seems to really care and always finds a way to get away with it. His life seems to go to pieces without him noticing it. On top of that he falls in love with the same girl as his best friend Opa (a passionate football referee). When Francesca becomes pregnant Daniel, being fully unprepared, gets in the position of having to take responsibilty for the first time in his life.
