Explore the secret world of the bodyguards who risk their lives to protect the rich, famous and powerful.
Bodyguards &amp; Protection Agents are the individuals on the front lines for the rich, famous, and powerful. These individuals deal with violence, stalkers, isolation, and potential loss of life every single day. Our film explores the lives of bodyguards; a world most people know nothing about. Showcasing Nelson Mandela's Bodyguards, Justin Bieber and his Director of Security, Whitey Bulger's Protector, 50 Cent &amp; Lil Wayne's Bodyguard, and Ambassador's under Blackwater.
