Alex and Selma are a couple in love on a trip to the heart of Bosnia and Herzegovina to discover more about each other. Lost and seemingly surrounded by land mines a pair of locals offer to guide them home.
Alec and Sasa are a couple in love on a trip to the heart of Bosnia and Herzegovina. When their car breaks down in the middle of the forest, they decide to look for help to the nearer village. During the trip, Sasa, a muslim survivor from the Balcanes war, feels distressed about the presence of a mysterious force which it seems is chasing them. Alec, her naif and young european boyfriend, tries to make her mind up about the situation. But Sasa hold herself on her thoughts and her amulet which will detonate the emergence of the mysterious force.
