Old love and new love collide when a tragic accident brings a woman's childhood sweetheart back into her life.
An artist ceases living a life of her own to care for her severely injured husband when a long-lost childhood friend attempts to help her find her own life again.
Abby and James were childhood sweethearts until James moves away and they lose touch. Abby doesn't see James for several years until he shows up at her gallery still carrying a torch for her. Unbeknownst to James Abby is engaged to be married to William. Even though it is obvious Abby still carries a torch for James as well she still marries William. One year later when tragedy strikes Abby is left to decide between the love she's found with William and the love she's always had for James.
