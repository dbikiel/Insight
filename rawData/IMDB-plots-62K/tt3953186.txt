A pandemic has taken place in the near future that has forced mankind into a desperate survival mode. An isolated family of three young sisters live out their days in this desolate new environment. They struggle to get by as each day food and resources become more scarce and their patience with each other wears thin. One day, a stranger finds their stronghold and the girls soon discover that everything they thought they knew was not what it seemed.
