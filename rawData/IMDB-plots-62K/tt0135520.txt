A sequel to 'Laterna ftoheia kai filotimo'. The 2 'laterna" (a portable barrel piano) players have spent all the reward and Pavlaras gets seriously sick. Petrakis tries to find Kaiti but she has divorced and moved from her past address. He is compelled to sell the laterna secretly. When Pavlaras recovers, he finds out about the sale and gets mad. However, they finally find Kaiti who helps them with their life, while they help her reunite with her husband, who does not even know they have a child together.
