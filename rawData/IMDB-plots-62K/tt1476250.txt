During World War I in 1918, a pigeon and his friends spring into action.
Our story of bravery and friendship takes place during WWI. The leading characters, Cher Ami, Sir Archibald, Venise, the little sparrow Tourbillon and the mouse Lindbergh live on a farm that is about to be turned into a military training post and communications centre for the Allies.
When they all decide to enlist as carrier pigeons, life on the farm is suddenly turned upside-down. Short on training, they are sent to help an American battalion trapped behind enemy lines..
