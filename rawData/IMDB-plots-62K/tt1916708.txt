31 years old, broke, single and lonely Yukio encounters four beautiful women suddenly appearing in his life. Will he be able to escape his "Moteki" status?
He is a nerd. He is a wimp. He has no confidence and, despite thousands of tweets, is followed by three accounts on Twitter. Yes, he is not exactly the winning type and he knows it. Then things begin to change suddenly. Different women want to be with him, follow him, like him, kiss him and even, gasp, love him. He is 'Moteki.' Does his newfound status have legs?
