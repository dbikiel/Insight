Carol Clayton is the daughter of a horse breeder at Saratoga. Though engaged to wealthy Hartley Madison, and disgusted by bookie Duke Bradley, her father owes Bradley a lot of money and Bradley takes a shine to her.
Brookdale horse farm, in the Clayton family for generations, is broke because of Frank Clayton's gambling, when news comes that Frank's daughter Carol plans to marry millionaire Hartley Madison. But matters are complicated because bookie Duke Bradley holds the deed to Brookdale, and Carol is determined to pay him off with no help from Hartley. And don't the sparks fly between Duke and Carol! The climax hangs on the result of a very close horse race...
