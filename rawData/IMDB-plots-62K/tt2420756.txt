When deadly beasts attack from the forest, it is up to a grizzled veteran to uncover what the residents of a secluded retirement community are hiding.
Ambrose McKinley, a cantankerous blind vet, moves into a retirement community only to learn the residents there have been dying, not from old age, but from dog attacks. After surviving his own encounter with a canine one night, Ambrose comes to believe the assailants are much more than mere dogs...
A normally quiet retirement community is plagued by mysterious animal attacks. When a blind grizzled war veteran Ambrose McKinley (Nick Damici) moves in, he discovers something sinister is behind them. When everyone dismisses what's going on, and won't help, Ambrose uses his military training and sets out to stop the beast before it's too late.
