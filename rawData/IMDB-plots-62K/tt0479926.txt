1985. A secret tree house. Two children dressed in animal costumes experiment to revive a bee. But they are searching for a way to bring back something much bigger.
"Hibernation" is a rare look into the hearts and minds of children.
Jason (Adam Arnold) and Sidney (Sonny Rooney) experiment with bees and batteries in an attempt to bring their friend, Robin (Adam Paroussos) back from his hibernation.
The film attempts to bring the viewer back to that moment when we realize what it means to be mortal.
