A bodyguard must go undercover to protect a tycoon's daughter from death threats before her big wedding day.
Texas-born tycoon Thompson hires an expensive security firm for his spoiled-rotten daughter Daisy's grand wedding at his Californian estate. fashion-obsessed airhead Daisy refuses 'goons', so the job has to be done by the only female bodyguard, Tanya, who must overcome her aversion of all things feminine to go undercover as bridesmaid. Suspects abound, notably among the silver spoon groom's frat gang, which includes one fake identity. Incidents occur.
