A group of mountaineers in the Scottish Highlands discover a kidnapped girl and are pursued by her captors.
A group of five mountaineers are hiking and climbing in the Scottish Highlands when they discover a young Serbian girl buried in a small chamber in the wilderness. They become caught up in a terrifying game of cat and mouse with the kidnappers as they try to get the girl to safety.
The mountain climbers Rob, Ed, Alison, Kate and Alex are best friends. While hiking in the Scottish Highlands, they find a vent pipe and a Serbian girl named Anna buried in the wilderness in a small compartment. They rescue Anna and split in two groups: Rob and Alison takes a shortcut to reduce the distance in 25 km climbing a difficult rock to go to the police and request a helicopter while Ed, Alex and Kate walks with Anna through a path along 48 km to the next village. But soon they are chased by the cruel kidnappers and they try to save their lives.
