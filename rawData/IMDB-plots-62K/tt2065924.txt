Amudha, a village girl visiting Chennai for the first time, falls in love with Gautham, a stranger, who shows her around the city. Kathiresan, a metal worker, is infatuated by Manimegalai, a nurse.
The film revolves around a group of passengers travelling in two different buses, each having their own past &amp; a reason for their travel. The most prominent among those is the love track between Kathiresan (Jai)- Manimegalai (Anjali) &amp; the other between Vinay (Saravanandh) &amp; Amudha (Ananya). Destiny has something in store for all of them, which changes their lives forever...
