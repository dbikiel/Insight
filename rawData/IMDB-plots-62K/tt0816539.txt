A troubled young man still mourning the childhood loss of his family now has to deal with his professor being turned into a monster.
As a child Jack Brooks witnessed the brutal murder of his family. Now a young man he struggles with a pestering girlfriend, therapy sessions that resolve nothing, and night classes that barely hold his interest. After unleashing an ancient curse, Jack's Professor undergoes a transformation into something not-quite- human, and Jack is forced to confront some old demons... along with a few new ones.
As a young boy Jack Brooks witnessed the brutal murder of his family by a monster, Years later he is suffering from anger issues blaming himself for not doing something about it. However Jack must become an unlikely hero and face his "demons" when his science professor accidently releases an ancient curse that threatens the world
