Story of a wife who has to deal with her lazy, immature and irresponsible husband.
A sweet woman is married to a man with two kids. The story features the satire of running a family as a woman when you're dealt with a husband who drinks and makes merry. It also shows a bittersweet tale of struggles the mom has to deal to send her kids to school while dealing with the absconded husband reflects the tale of a lot of families. The whole movies is cast in a comical tune that it reverberates well.
