It seems Lardimaa lives in all women's dream life. Snow white wedding dress, out of this world wedding reception, big and expensive house, high-end furniture, wealthy caring husband. Husband is rich, but seems caring in front of everyone's eyes. Takes her everywhere and buys her the world. In reality, all she wants to do is to get away from her husband. After some incident she ends up in a prison and meets the prison guard, who also is not happy with her life. Lardimaa's struggle helps the guard woman to see her life in reality and helps Lardimaa to get out of prison and be free from her manipulating, sadistic husband and takes Lardimaa's place in the prison.
