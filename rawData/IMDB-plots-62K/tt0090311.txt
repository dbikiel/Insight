A recovering substance-abusing country singer and his brother exact revenge on the singer's manager, who has cheated him out of a substantial amount of cash throughout the years.
Joe Hawkins is not in good spirits. A long time drug-abuser and alcoholic, he sobers up with help from his brother Tom. When Joe discovers that his manager, Leon Redden, has cheated him out of $8,000,000 over the years, Joe, Tom, and a few friends set out to show Leon that karma is a mother, and that what goes around comes around.
