Ahmad is an army commander who must confront the enemy at the gates of the Khorramshahr. But his fate is determined far from the Iranian border.
Ahmad Motevaselian is a commander who defends his country from invading forces, but his mysterious fate is ultimately determined far from his homeland. Through genuine audio excerpts and entirely narrated by his comrades and family members, this unique biopic meticulously recounts the extraordinary life-story of one of the Revolutionary Guards' most prominent commanders.
