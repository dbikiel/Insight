A comedy about a civil registry official whose job is to register newlyweds and who just discovered that her husband has been cheating on her for 3 months, her daughter met a guy she doesn't approve of, and her life has suddenly become a fuss of the fusses.
Boris Ivanovich, already a middle-aged man, after many years of family life, was carried away by another woman and left the family. His ex-wife Marina Petrovna, the head of the registry office, is going through a separation. To escape from the problems, she decides to go on a trip by ship to Kizhi, but is late for the voyage. Her daughter Natasha, thinking that her mother is in Kizhi, decides to leave for Novgorod to her fiance. Not finding her daughter at home, the mother goes to look for her in Novgorod. Her ex-husband is also looking for daughter. Then the circumstances are such that Borya understands that he is losing, leaving the beloved wife.
