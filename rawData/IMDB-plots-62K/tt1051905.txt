Paris, August: Driven by his lust, Richard O. explores the sinuous mysteries of eroticism and the women who populate the summer months.
Driven by lust and determined to explore his erotic obsessions, Richard O. takes a leave of absence from his current relationship to wander the sun soaked streets of Paris and traverse all boarders of sex. His notions of love growing increasingly vague, the sexually insatiable wanderer searches for himself by engaging in everything from the classic rituals of lovemaking to the highly exotic positions of the Kama Sutra. In using sex as a means of connecting with the people around him, He experiences both the pleasures and problems of leading such a lustful lifestyle.
