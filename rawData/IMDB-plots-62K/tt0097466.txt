A young man wants to join the police force. When he fails, in frustration he joins a smuggling gang instead.
Guru's ambition is to become an upright Police officer, but unluckily he is not getting selected. His sole strength is his lady love Uma. As Guru's ambition fails, he turns to alcohol and criminality. He finally joins a smuggling gang run by gangster Vicky to take revenge on the Police force which thwarted his ambition. But he is shocked to see that Uma is also working for the same gang. One day, Vicky receives a confidential message about an undercover police officer in his gang.
