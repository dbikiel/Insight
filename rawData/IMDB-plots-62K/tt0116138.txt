A young man, with the help of his unsuspecting roommates, hikes through the Canadian wilderness in search of Sasquatch.
Donner and his roomates are out of money and about to get evicted from their home. So as a diversion from their situation, he invites them to his father's cabin in the Canadian wilderness. They all go along, but soon learn that there's no cabin - he's really trying to find Sasquatch.
Donnor (Jason Lee) and his roommates are poor and about to get evicted when Donnor starts to get these visions of Sasquatch and has suspicions of a community of them living in his local woods, he tricks his roommates into going on a hike while he's on the hunt
