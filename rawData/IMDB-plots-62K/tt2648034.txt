The story of how Rasool, a Muslim falls in love with a Christian girl, Anna and how her family's strong disapproval makes their relationship change for the worse.
A realistic and practical movie about two lovers of different religions;and the life of people around them, giving importance to each of their stories,told in a narrative mode by a marine who returns home for vacation but ends up playing an important role in the lovers' life and also finds his lost teenage love.Not a movie with too much humor or action,but one which stand close to real life.
