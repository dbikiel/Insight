Contemporary Russia. A young couple finds a great deal on a used BMW. Unfortunately, it's haunted.
A family quarrel tragically ends up with a murdered wife. The husband, after cutting her body into pieces, hides it in the car boot, hoping to get rid of it later. Despite the attempts to mislead the police, he is shortly arrested. Andrey and Olga can't believe their luck! They just made an excellent bargain in buying a second hand car, a car they always dreamt of. They don't realize yet that they got into a trap! There is someone else in the car... someone who will turn their journey into a terrifying hell.
