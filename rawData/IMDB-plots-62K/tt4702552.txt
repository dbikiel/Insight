A desperate son reconnects with his estranged father to ask an unspeakable favor that will change their lives forever.
A game of twisted dares will reunite an indebted son with his unconcerned father for a "Would you Dare" challenge for money. Of course, the reward for the desperate son's unreasonable demand is more than tempting--and as a result--the ludicrous proposition is put on the table. However, after the father's initial shock, human nature takes control, and the bar is raised once more. So, without further ado, let the games begin.
