A spoiled rich man doesn't take it well when he's rejected by an ambulance paramedic. He doesn't give up there but uses his parents' wealth to get to ride with her in the ambulance hoping that it will eventually lead to a date etc.
Lilly, a hard working EMT, is only focused on her job and getting into medical school. So when wealthy slacker Jeff enters her life it throws her off course. At first, Lilly wants nothing to do with Jeff or his sense of entitlement and lack of direction. As she spends more time with him, she starts to realize that you can't always judge a book by its cover.
A talented ambitious female paramedic struggling to get into medical school and being bumped off the top of the list by "rich families who donate a wing to get their kid to the top of the list" meets a rich pretentious man determined to show her a different side of who he is.
