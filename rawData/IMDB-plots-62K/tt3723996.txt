In the year 2150, Johnny, a lazy Space Delivery Man, must deliver a package on a planet he does not fully understand.
It's 2150. There are all sorts of Aliens living throughout space. Johnny is a Space Delivery Man who travels to different planets to deliver packages. Johnny is lazy and his only desire is to sleep in his autopilot spaceship. when the spaceship arrives at the destination, all he has to do is simply deliver the box. However, it never goes as planned. Johnny encounters strange and bizarre planets and always seems to cause trouble on his delivery route.
The year is 2150, and Johnny Express, the laziest space-delivery man in the entire universe, travels from one planet to another (mainly in auto-pilot), delivering packages of all sorts; great and small, tiny and grand. This time, a minute, almost microscopic parcel needs to be handed over to a mysterious recipient located in an exotic and strange star, somewhere in the deep, cold space. How should Johnny handle it?
