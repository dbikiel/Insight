Raymond Dabney returns to his family after trouble with the law. He convinces the sheriff to give him a job watching the house and furniture of widow Crystal Wetherby without knowing she is engaged to his brother.
Taylor is smitten by Harlow, Harlow is broke. What ensues is hilarious, very witty. The back and forth between Taylor and Harlow spews gems. "The zest in quest" could have become Harry Chapin's: "It's not the getting, but the going that's good." Do not miss this one.
