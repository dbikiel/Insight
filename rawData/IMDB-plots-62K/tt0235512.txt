The Rockmores set out on a family road trip with an uninvited Kel stashed in the trunk. While camping out in the woods, Kenan has a scary encounter with a mysterious, shadowy figure...
When the Rockmore family car breaks down in the middle of nowhere, Kenan and Kel go to the nearest town to find help. Little do they know the town they entered is the home of the dreaded Headless Knight who plans to use their heads for his own!
