200 Cartas is a romantic comedy about the life of a Puerto Rican born in NY. One night, Raul meets Maria, a young Puerto Rican woman visiting New York. The night of their encounter, something dramatic happens and they are suddenly separated. Struck by love at first sight, he travels to Puerto Rico in search of this beautiful girl. This adventure will have him traveling around the island in search of his love.
Raul (Lin Manuel Miranda), a young newyorican man meets a beautiful Puerto Rican girl, Maria Sanchez (Mayra Matos), in a club in New York City. Before he can get to know her a fight breaks and they get separated. He can't stop thinking about her and decides to go to Puerto Rico with his good friend Juan, (Jaime Camil) to look for her. Upon arriving he discovers that there are over 200 Maria Sanchez in the phone book! So, he decides to write 200 letters to find his dream girl. His quest will take Raul and Juan in a comical journey throughout Puerto Rico where they'll meet Yolanda (Dayanara Torres) a new friend that will help them. Will Raul find what he is looking for?
