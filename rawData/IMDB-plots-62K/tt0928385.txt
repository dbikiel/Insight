Based on characters designed by Katsuhiro Otomo, 3 bike racing teenagers born on the Moon uncover a terrible secret about the fate of their original homeworld earth and seek to uncover the truth from Eden, the organization that governs the moon colony.
In 2041 A.D., the first space colony was made on the moon. After a freak cataclysm devastated human civilization on Earth. The republic of the moon created a doomed megalopolis known as Eden. 226 years have past since then, now it's the year 2267. A boy named Takeru, spent his life engaging in hover-craft races against rival street gangs of the 23rd century. But he was unaware that he'll embark in the journey of unearthing the origins of Eden.
