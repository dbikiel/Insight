When a new system for mapping DNA called the Platinum Data is developed for all the residents of Japan, everyone is convinced it will create a better, saver society, free from crime. But when the creator of the system is accused of murder, he must prove his innocence and unravel a mystery at the center of which lies the Platinum Data itself.
A scientist developed a DNA matching database program to help police with crime busting leads but later got involved in a crime himself.
