By accident Dagwood discovers a non-flammable paint. Bad guys Dillon and Stack steal it before he can give it to his boss Radcliffe. To show off his invention, Dagwood paints Radcliffe's house with it and disgraced when the house burns down. Blondie discovers the culprits and saves the day.
Dagwood invents, by accident, a fire-proof paint which he is sure will win for his boss, Mr. Radcliffe, the contract for the new school building, Just before Radcliffe demonstrates the paint on his summer home, rival contractors switch regular paint for the fireproof paint and the home goes up in flames when ignited. Rollo, boy genius, discovers the paint switch and who pulled it. Blondie gets a job with the crooks and sets out to right the wrong done to her ever-loving husband.
