A young man goes from China to Hollywood on short notice to find his girlfriend. He ends up connected with two others in his quest, which gets much more complicated than he envisioned.
Part buddy road movie, part romantic comedy, part action film, part heist flick and 100% the adventure of their lives as our heroes fall down the bizarre rabbit hole of Hollywood where they will face everything from unstoppable hitmen to post-Apocalyptic bikers to movie stars with dark secrets and even a talking pig. A movie where real life and reel life collide to give birth to an epic cross-cultural adventure where anything is possible because after all, this is Hollywood!
