Episodes from the last few years in the life of iconic Silesian poet Rafal Wojaczek. His provocative and scandalous life as well as tragic premature death made him a true literary legend.
The short and tragic life of Silesian poet Rafal Wojaczek, who used to live the way he wrote and to write the way he lived. He constantly rebelled against the dull 1960s reality by causing scandals, drinking heavily, getting naked in public and leaving restaurants through the window. His legendary poems and suicidal death at the age of 27 made him a literary icon of the doomed generation.
