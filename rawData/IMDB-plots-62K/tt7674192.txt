A tale of a long-legged bird's journey of blissful joy, inescapable greed and the accidental discovery of the consequences when too much is not enough.
A short 2D animated film set in a swamp about a seemingly cute heron obsessively waiting for the perfect prey, a mystical red glowing fish, to jump out. Once it finally catches it, the heron's true predatory nature is revealed, for which it faces karmic judgment.
