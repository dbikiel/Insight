It begins as a contest of strength the challenge of competition luring fighters from far away to a hilltop castle. Deathstalker leads the combatants but only to discover that the winners are disappearing one by one. Now, in his last and greatest battle, Deathstalker must defend his remaining comrades, his life, and his newfound love in battle against an evil army of stone warriors and the wicked queen who created them.
After retrieving the lost fragments of Erendor's magical gem in Deathstalker and the Warriors from Hell (1988), the masterful thief and dauntless barbarian warrior, Deathstalker, finds himself up against formidable competitors in the impregnable castle of the demonic sorceress, Queen Kana. Along with the fearless she-warrior, Dionara, Deathstalker must fight wave after wave of ambitious champions in Kana's savage tournament of death, unbeknownst to him that evil forces are at work, and a sinister scheme is afoot. Now, the rules of the game have changed, and the queen's indestructible stone-guardians demand blood. Will Deathstalker emerge victorious in the ferocious Match of the Titans?
