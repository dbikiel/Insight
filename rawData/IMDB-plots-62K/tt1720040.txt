A shepherd leaves behind his life in Mexico after coming across a mysterious key chain. Will the key chain lead him to his own paradise or will the journey be all for nothing?
The story of Jacinto Medina, a young Mexican herder who is bored with his life herding sheep and dreams of moving north. Jacinto decides to leave his life behind and embark upon a personal odyssey when he stumbles upon a mysterious keychain. Will the key chain lead Jacinto to his own paradise or will the journey be all for nothing?
Jacinto Medina, un joven de 21 años, esta aburrido de su vida como pastor en el norte de México. Encuentra un llavero en el suelo, viéndolo como una señal, emprenderá un viaje que lo hará recorrer miles de kilómetros.
