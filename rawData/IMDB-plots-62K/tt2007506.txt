Three down-to-earth guys, record songs and reach out for alternatives to their ordinary lives.
Three down-to-earth guys, working on day-to-day jobs, recording songs and reaching out for alternatives beyond ordinary.. This is an extraordinary fuzzomentary about a band called Truckfighters. TRUCKFIGHTERS, a film by Joerg Steineck and Christian Maciejewski, provides uncommon insight into the double-tracked lifestyle of a young determined and in a way untypical fuzz rock band.
