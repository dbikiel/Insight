A disturbing psychological thriller, that engages the audience to the point that it is always caught off guard, thus changing the point of view of the two main performers.
The Elevator is set in the confines of an elevator where the host of a famous TV Game Show is attacked and tortured by an apparently demented woman who claims that the kidney transplant he paid for resulted in the death of her son. The elevator is in a New York building where Jack Tramell lives. The woman, Kathryn, has studied her sons revenge meticulously. It is a quiet evening of the Labour Day weekend. Kathryn is cynical and scientific in carrying out her plan. But is she insane or is there truth in her accusations? Is Tramell guilty, or is he caught up in a deathly game not of his making?
The Elevator is a drama and a psychological thriller, that keeps the audience on the edge of his seat through a series of sudden twists and nail biting tension.
