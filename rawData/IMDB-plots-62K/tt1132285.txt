An obsessed cop is on the trail of a serial killer prowling the streets of Buffalo, N.Y. but when his teenage daughter disappears, he drops any professional restraint to get the killer.
Detective Mike Fletcher, a rugged and obsessive police officer, and his partner Kelsey Walker, are on the trail of a serial killer who prowls the streets targeting young streetwalkers. When his teenage daughter disappears, Fletcher discovers that the killer has kidnapped her after mistaking her for a prostitute. Fletcher's obsession goes into overdrive when he drops all professional restraint to get the killer and save his daughter.
This Thriller is about Detective Mike Fletcher (John Cusack) and his partner Kelsey Walker (Jennifer Carpenter from DEXTER) as they are on the track of a serial killer who targets prostitutes. Every time they get close to the killer it seems like he is a step ahead of them. Meanwhile Detective Mike's daughter Abby is giving him problems at home by not doing as his wife asks each time, and the daughter also is seeing an older college boy which is more trouble. Abby goes to meet her boyfriend only to find herself captured by the same killer that her father is after. The case becomes more personal for Detective Mike and his partner, but will they find Abby before she becomes the killers next victim?
Based on a true story, The Factory follows an obsessed cop, along with his partner, on the trail of a killer responsible for a series of vigilante style murders and disappearances in Buffalo, NY. When his teenage daughter goes missing, fearing the worst, he loses all professional restraint trying to catch the killer, never suspecting that killing is actually the lesser evil of this man's offenses.
