About two guys working at a shops's stock. Antero get involved with Karita, who gets accidentally pregnant. Antero wants abort the child, but Karita, knowing about Anteros illegal actions at work, threatens to blackmail him to get him to form a family with her. Antero agrees to keep the baby. Later on, the boss finds out that one of his employees is stealing from the stock. Antero chooses to blame his co-worker to keep his job and provide the baby and Karita easier life.
