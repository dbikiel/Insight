The love between Alex, a mature successful advertising agent, and Niki, now twenty, continues to deepen and blossom. It has now been three years since they vowed to love each other forever before their special rendezvous lighthouse. Despite their age difference, Alex is convinced Niki is the woman he wants to marry. And Niki, in the beginning, is overjoyed. Yet, as the crucial date approaches, mounting doubts, fears - and temptations too - suddenly make Niki do the unthinkable. She calls off the wedding. Far from being the end of the couple's love story, however, this could unexpectedly be the tender, courageous and amusing beginning of Niki and Alex's "impossible" dream come true...
