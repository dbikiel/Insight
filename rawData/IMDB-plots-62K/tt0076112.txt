A desperate young woman goes undercover to infiltrate the brutal warden Ilsa's mental health facility for the sexually deviant women, after the mysterious disappearance of her sister.
Ilsa, now a vicious warden, runs a mental-hospital for young women. A girl deliberately "checks" in to the hospital to find out what has happened to her sister who stayed there. Meanwhile Ilsa and one of the guards are forcing the inmates to have sex with male prisoners, filming them and selling it as pornoflicks.
At Las Palomas mental health facility for the sexually deviant women, the brutal tall warden Ilsa rules the institution with an iron fist, performing horrors on the hapless inmates. However, when a young patient disappears, her sister, Abbie, goes undercover by willingly incarcerating herself with the aid of Dr Arcos. There, Abbie will meet Juana, the hospital's most fearful girl inmate, unbeknownst to her that both Juana and Warden Ilsa have some sinister plans of their own. Will Abbie escape Ilsa's prison of torment alive?
