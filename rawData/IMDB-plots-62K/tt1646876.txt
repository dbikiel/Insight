Abby's made a 16 wishes list for her 16th birthday. She gets 16 magic candles - 1 wish per hour. Things are fine in the beginning.
A 16-year-old girl prepares a list of 16 wishes for eight years, hoping they will come true on her 16th birthday. A fairy comes to give her 16 candles that make the 16 wishes come true. Her wishes go the wrong way and are permanent when midnight comes. She has one final wish to reverse her previous wishes.
Shy Jay Kepler (16) always helps his classmate and neighbor Abby Jensen, whom he has a serious crush on, even in her bitter rivalry with rich neighbor and classmate Krista Cook. Against jay's warnings, Abby greedily accepts from fairy Celeste and uses hourly 16 wish candles to make come true her sweet sixteen wish list on the big day, which started miserably with a wasp plague rendering the Jensen house inhabitable. Although each comes true, the crucial ones backfire big time, robbing her of all that really matters. However in the process she comes to appreciate and reevaluate, just in time for a last chance twist.
