It is the final year of high school and students are studying, taking exams, examining their options after graduation and, oh, catching their teacher having an affair with another teacher. What to do? Well, demand the same treatment of course. Never mind, that the male teacher is married and has a wife who is expecting. Sleeping with the teacher is great, but so is sleeping with a fellow student. What to do?
Tsumugi, a sensual schoolgirl gets swept up in a steamy lust triangle between her horny teacher, Katagiri and her shy classmate, Kosuke. She is a typical Japanese teenager: pretty, curious, and adventurous. She seduces her teacher while exploring the more sordid side of sexuality with Kosuke. When both relationships become too intense to maintain, she must make an important decision. Will she choose the flutter or first love, or the thrill of being libertinism?
