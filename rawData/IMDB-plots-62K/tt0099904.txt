Fact-based story of a Louisiana priest accused of molesting young parishioners, and of the family of one of his victims, caught between their loyalty to their son and to their Church.
This is a fact based story about a Catholic priest with a history of molestations of minor aged boys,a history that the church hierarchy has kept hidden until it is discovered that the priest has been molesting the boys of 9 families. The family of one of the young victims is shocked when they learn the church is more interested in paying the families hush money rather than the protection of the boys.
