A beautiful yet troubled orphan must battle her demons of alcoholism and self-destruction when she becomes an international singing star overnight.
Winter Rose has survived being orphaned as an infant and being moved around from orphanage to orphanage. Now on her own she struggles with her abandonment and finds comfort in her one true passion, music. When legendary singer Rachal Love is diagnosed with cancer she launches a world wide search to find her successor. Will Winter be able to conquer her demons of self doubt and face the pressures of the world wide stage? A WINTER ROSE is the inspiring and timeless tale of how the human spirit can triumph when faced with insurmountable odds.
