A young man returns home from college to find his father remarried, until the sudden murder of his father leaves everyone suspect.
Young university student, Holden returns home from college. While he was away his father, Mason married to a young sexy woman, Lana. He embarks on a passionate affair with his stepmother that leads to the murder of his father. Now seduced and then blackmailed by his stepmother, he must prove his innocence in his father's murder-a difficult task as twists and turns abound in this highly charged thriller.
