When Ninas husband goes off for a few weeks to work, she meets and starts seeing a photographer. This story is told as an interview by a journalist writing about adultery who is talking with both Nina and the photographer in separate interviews. Through him, we learn much about the couple and their attitudes on adultery.
A reporter who writes a book about infidelity interviews various people about their personal experiences in this matter. One of them is Nina, a young ordinary girl who has a "different story" to tell. While her husband was away on a 3-week business trip, she met a man who soon became her lover. With him, she learned things about true love that she didn't even "touch" inside her marriage.
A lonely young woman decides to kindle her passions in a fling with a married photographer after feeling challenged by her own best friend's affair.
