To equip the American zoos with as many animals as possible, a cruel trio of big-game hunters team up with an unexpected ally, threatening the African fauna. Will Tarzan allow the fiendish huntress to pillage the jungle?
A shortage of zoo animals after World War II brings beautiful animal trainer Tanya, her financial backer and her cruel trail boss to the jungle. After negotiating a quota with the native king, they take more animals than allowed. Tarzan intervenes.
At King Farrod's birthday celebration, the great animal trainer, Tanya Rawlins; her financial supporter, Carl Marley, and the big-game hunter, Paul Weir, are granted limited access to the region's magnificent fauna. Intent on equipping the American zoos with as many animals as possible, the ruthless trio team up with an unexpected ally, as strange machinations threaten not only the habitat's rich and balanced biodiversity but also the kingdom itself. Will Tarzan allow the fiendish huntress and her invading opportunists to pillage the jungle?
