An adaptation of the Batman: Hush storyline, by Jeph Loeb and Jim Lee.
BATMAN: HUSH is a thrilling mystery of action, intrigue, and deception based on the New York Times bestseller of the same name penned by Jeph Loeb (Batman: The Long Halloween) and illustrated by comics superstar Jim Lee (All Star Batman &amp; Robin, The Boy Wonder). Batman sets out to discover the identity of a mysterious mastermind using the Joker, Riddler, Ra's al Ghul, and the Dark Knight's other enemies and allies as pawns in a plan to wreak havoc.
