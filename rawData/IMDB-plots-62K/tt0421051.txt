Evil assassins want to kill Daniel Kublbock, the third runner up for the German Idols.
Daniel, as you may have not known, is an active and proud homosexual as you can tell from the "make-up" on his lips. In this movie, Daniel tries to place his magical finger into places it doesn't belong. Watch as Daniel goes on his thrilling and shocking adventure to find a YMCA that will let him in the door to play with the children. As he travels throughout the east, he encounters many police officers and trigger-happy rednecks attempting to stop him from placing his finger where it does not belong. Enjoy!
