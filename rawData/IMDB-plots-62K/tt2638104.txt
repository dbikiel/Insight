A drive on the highway lands two friends in serious trouble.
Karthi (Sugan), is a playboy who seems to have had a style makeover with his trendy clothes, new hairstyle and colored eye lens. Despite having a gorgeous girlfriend Priyanka (Hansika), Sugan flirts with all the pretty women he meets and they too, are besotted by him. Sugan, on a journey with his childhood friend Parasu (Premji) stops at a roadside eatery for biriyani (food), his weakness. But they gets into a problem after having the dish.
