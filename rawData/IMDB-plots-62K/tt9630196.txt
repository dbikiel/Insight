Documentary on the challenges and various remedies for potholes in U.K. roads. But it probably applies to paved roads anywhere.
Potholes are driving Britain mad. They cause rage, rows and road works, costing billions of pounds to repair, and are responsible for injuring hundreds of people each year. The road network is being pushed to the max and for the workers on the frontline, it can be like going into battle. Some councils say they're at financial breaking point, struggling to cope with the pothole problem while 'pothole vigilantes' are taking matters into their own hands.
