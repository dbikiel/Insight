A young couple are tricked into going into a sinister house where a coven of devil-worshipers waits for them.
Ana and Andres, an urbanite couple living in Madrid, leave their apartment for a pleasant day around the city with their dog. They cross paths with Bruno and Berta, a strange couple who invite them to their foreboding country estate. A storm hits that evening and the two stay overnight. The couples engage in a bit of harmless communication with spirits via ouija board, but soon past conflicts arise Ana had an affair with Andres' brother, and Berta criticizes Bruno over his attempted suicide. This is only the beginning of all the horrors that will haunts them in the house.
