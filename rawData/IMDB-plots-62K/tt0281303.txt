A funny musical comedy based on the famous theater play 'Servant of Two Masters' by Carlo Goldoni.
The cheerful and tireless joker Truffaldino is hired by two masters who live in different rooms of the same hotel. Soon it turns out that both masters are not only know each other, but even were the bride and groom in the past, but for the time being do not know that the other is near. And the tireless Truffaldino has time to serve everyone, and even find a bride for himself.
