Follows the struggles through life and relationships of Komatsu Nana and Osaki Nana.
Nana and Nana begin together, but drift apart due to the men in their lives. When Nana 'Hachiko' is forced into making the only choice that makes sense to her she hurts her boyfriend whom she leaves behind. In the meanwhile, Black Stones have a record deal and surge in popularity to rival Trapnest.
