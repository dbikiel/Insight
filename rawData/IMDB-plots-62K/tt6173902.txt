Doctor Strange and a team of monsters must help the Hulk overcome his dream self in order to defeat Nightmare on Halloween night.
The Halloween Nightmare is bent on conquering our waking world by crossing through the Dream Dimension, and converting each dreamer into a monster. Can Doctor Strange, Hulk and the Howling Commandos hold the line and put an end to his nefarious scheme?
Nightmare, the evil ruler of a Dream Dimension, is determined to conquer Earth and take control of humanity by turning them into monsters. Can Doctor Strange and Hulk, with the help of a spooky version of the Howling Commandos of S.H.I.E.L.D., put an end to his heinous scheme?
