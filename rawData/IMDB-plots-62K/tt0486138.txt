When a man fires a hotel maid for stealing, the woman's son gets his revenge.
Achille De Bellis is a luxury hotel chain manager. The hotel chain is owned by his wife Gigliola and her father Guglielmo. As the silver wedding anniversary gets close every certainty in life starts fading as Achille meets Orfeo, the son of an employee of his hotel that was fired by Achille for allegedly stealing. Since Orfeo believes that his mother was unjustly fired he decides to make Achilles life miserable
