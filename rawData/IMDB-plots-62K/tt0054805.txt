A crusty, eccentric priest recruits 3 reluctant convicts to help him rescue a children's leper colony from a Pacific island menaced by a smoldering volcano.
3 convicts enroute to Tahiti are put to work at a children's hospital when their plane makes an unexpected stop. Father Perreau is to get off and replace another church officer. Once on the island, a volcano begins to erupt, and the governor orders an evacuation.All must work together if any are to survive.
