The thing that waits under your bed, hides in the closet, stalks your dreams - is waiting for you. At least Emma Wright knew that at the age of 10 when she was wrongly accused of murdering her sister. Emma knew it was the Stickman. After years of isolation, she finally has her demons under control and is released to live her life again - Those childhood demons put to rest, but the Stickman has other plans for Emma.
