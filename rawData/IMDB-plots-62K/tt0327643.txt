A jilted photographer sets off on a mission to get back at her philandering model boyfriend and along the way discovers that not all love is created equal.
A modern day Cinderella story which sees disaster prone Rebecca embark on a journey in search of true love. Betrayed by her boyfriend, Richard and following a palm reader's psychic prophecy, Rebecca goes in search of her one true soul mate.
Two-bit LA photographer Rebecca Sommers fails to keep her classy boy-friend, super-sex model Richard Hunting, satisfied, so she ends up catching him having sex with a younger girl. Walking out she forgets her equipment, which he thrashes. Instead of giving her only devoted admirer, gentle John, a chance, she hopes to make Richard jealous.
