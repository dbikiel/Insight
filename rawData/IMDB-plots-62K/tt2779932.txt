The mad journey of a rebellious princess who ventures to work at a brothel to find the tattooed intruder she met on a fateful night.
Princess Sakura is attacked by a man, which turns her on. She cannot see her attacker's face, but does catch a glimpse of his tattoo signifying he is a thug and a criminal. She is horny for the man now and searches for him. In order to find him and be close to his manly way she gets the same tattoo and becomes a prostitute. The man who attacked the beautiful female has his own reasons for staying in the shadows. He is being sought by others as well.
