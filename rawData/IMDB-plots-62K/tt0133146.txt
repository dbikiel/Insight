Tori Spelling stars in this suspense comedy about three college students who find themselves in need of some fast money. With more ambition than experience in criminal enterprise, they decide to kidnap a young woman from a wealthy family. Along the way, they make practically every mistake imaginable, and just when they think they've finally worked the bugs out of their plan and have the girl in their clutches, they discover that they've kidnapped the wrong woman, who proves to be a lot more trouble than they expected. Sean Devine, Danny Strong, and Mark Burgess co-star as the hapless would-be criminals.
