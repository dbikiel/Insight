Swanand Gaddamwa comes to a small village Kusavde to get hold of a bull who was once considered holy by the villagers.
Swanand Gaddamwar a forest officer is appointed by the government to get hold of Valu a wild bull in the small village of Kusavde, "Valu", is actually a holy, consecrated bull which is the responsibility of the village. It is allowed to roam free and is the responsibility of the entire village. But lately, Valu becomes very aggressive and is blamed for every single act of chaos and destruction that happens in and around the village, Swanand feels ashamed that he is assigned this task by the government as he was always known to catch wild animals like Tigers and Leopards,Swanand reaches the village with his brother Sameer who wants to shoot a documentary and finds this subject interesting,Jeevan is a asked by Sarpanch to guide Swanand in what ever help he requires to catch Valu ,Jeevan and Swanand become good friends start meeting people and know the full history of Valu.Sarpanch and Abha are two village leaders who prefer to be one above the other and everyone wants to have the credits of catching Valu.
A Forest officer send in a small village to catch a wild bull. He met different people and with help of them he tries to catch the Bull.
