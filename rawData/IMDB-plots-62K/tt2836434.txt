A chronic philanderer falls victim to an extortion scheme when he is abducted by four masked strangers demanding retribution for his sins.
Alex is deeply in love with Liam. Liam is secretly married, and Alex isn't his only girl on the side. But when she finds out the details of her lovers other escapades, she recruits two additional marks and her brother Kip to kidnap her philandering ex and exact her revenge. The kidnapping is a success, but as the team waits for daybreak, complications arise as Liam tries to escape, and an unseen private detective gets caught in the act. Loyalties shift as four seemingly-well-intentioned friends must wait out the night, and each other, clinging to their bid for wealth as their successful extortion scheme goes horribly wrong.
