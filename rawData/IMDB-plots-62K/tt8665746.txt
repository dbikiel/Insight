Believing his girlfriend loves him even after being married, a man waits for her return and in the process ignites violence.
Shiva (Kartikeya) is a village youth who falls for a city bred girl Indu (Payal). As a passionate romance brews, Shiva falls madly in love with her, but things don't turn out as expected. Indu got married to someone else and departs to USA ,but Shiva waits 3 years for her in mad hope of uniting with her. She returns to their village one day, but does Shiva get her back?
