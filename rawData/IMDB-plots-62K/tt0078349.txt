A prince falls in love with a princess cursed to change into a swan by day, but their love is threatened by the sorcerer who keeps her imprisoned.
The hero, Prince Siegfried, is out riding one day with his friends when he spies amidst swans on a lake a particularly eye-catching one with a crown on its head, the familiar Princess Odette. The princess is charmed by an evil wizard Rothbart who is crazy about marrying Odette. Handsome Prince Siegfried together with two merry squirrels struggle to defeat the evil wizard to complete this classic fairy tales. The movie features original Tschaikovsky music.
Brimming with intense sentiments and Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky's thrilling music, Swan Lake commences with the awed Prince Siegfried's first encounter with a dainty and mysteriously alluring swan adorned with a golden crown. Compelled to follow this magnificent and noble cygnet, the young aristocrat will soon discover Princess Odette's cruel story and the terrible fate that awaits her after the shape-shifting sorcerer Rothbart's vitriolic curse. Like a priceless trophy, the invincible magician keeps the helpless lady locked up in a cold and dank room in his horrible castle; however, no obstacle can come between the fervent lovers. Can true love triumph over the dark forces of evil?
