Private detectives Mr and Mrs John Hillman visits a friend in the country. While there they track down a murderer who is disguised as a ghost.
Private detective John Hillman and his wife Kajsa take a break from their crime fighting careers and go on a vacation to a small town located at the Swedish countryside. But they soon get drawn into a mystery surrounding the strange disappearance of a young girl. The locals claim that she has been taken by a ghost, called the lady in black. But does the ghost really exist? And what happened to the girl?
