A washed-up luchador and a super-spy investigate Nazi zombies, a nefarious scientist, and a stripper with a Satanic birthmark.
The story follows the adventures of El Superbeasto, a washed-up Mexican luchador, and his sultry sidekick and sister Suzi-X as they confront an evil villain by the name of Dr. Satan. The adventure, set in the mythic world of Monsterland, also has a character named Murray the robot.
El Superbeasto--the masked luchador, washed-up adult film star, actor, and movie director--teams up with his gun-toting, one-eyed undercover agent sister, Suzi X, to stop the unholy union of the diabolical megalomaniac, Dr Satan, with the voluptuous stripper, Velvet von Black. Now, a hairy ape and a legion of undead Nazi bikers stand in the way of the intrepid brother/sister duo, as the future of humankind is hanging by a thread. Will El Superbeasto fulfil his destiny?
