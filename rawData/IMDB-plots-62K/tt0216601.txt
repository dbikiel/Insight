Life's not easy when you're a six-legged critter. Just ask Thang, Rose and Didi, three ant sisters trying to make it in one tough neighborhood. Join these intrepid anthropoids as they help the young Queen Jo build a new ant hill while avoiding the sinister geckos and keeping Sal Caterpillar out of trouble. The adventure never stops when you're a quarter inch long - in the underbrush.
