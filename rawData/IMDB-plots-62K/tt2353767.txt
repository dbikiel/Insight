Rebecca is one of the world's top war photographers. She must weather a major emotional storm when her husband refuses to put up with her dangerous life any longer. He and their young daughters need Rebecca, who, however, loves both her family and her work...
She's dressed in a burka taking photographs of a woman being outfitted with a bomb around her waist hidden inside her burka. She asks to ride along and continue "telling" the story. After a few kilometers she asks to get out just after the car is stopped by local militia. The bomber is detained inside the van and after being jostled the bomb explodes injuring our heroine photographer and many around the van including many children. She awakens in hospital in her country reunited with her normal life with her kids and husband where she faces the truth about her highly risky career choices!
