A drama-documentary telling the story of the celebrated gathering in Geneva, 1816 which led to the creation of both Frankenstein and the first modern vampire story.
Dramatised Documentary looking into how the legendary Lord Byron try's to escape scandal and his fame. Leads to him staying in a villa in Geneva. Staying at the villa were also friend. One dark and stormy night. The friends read ghost stories. A challenge of who could write a better ghost story than each other is thrown down. Which lead to the creation of Frankenstein.
