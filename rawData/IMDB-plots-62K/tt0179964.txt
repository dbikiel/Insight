Woman runs away having the company of her daughter, the ultimate brat, in a journey full of bizarre events.
Sérgio and Felicidade arrive at a hotel in Rio de Janeiro, with their teenage daughter, Betinha, discussing the relationship. A fight that culminates in the wife attacking the husband with a razor. Believing her husband is dead, she runs away with Betinha back to São Paulo. A journey that becomes a game of manipulation and violence.
