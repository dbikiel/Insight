Mousten, Momo and Abdelkrim spend their time letting their lives slip by. Until the day they discover an auditions advertisement in a porn magazine. From their standpoint of no girls and no money, being a porn star seems like the best job in the world.
Mousten, Abdelkrim and Momo are three childhood friends who have always lived in their city of Melun.
Caps pressed the head to the feet and sneakers, all three have the same problem: desperate singles, they spend their days elapse their lives, without motivation or ambition.
Until they find an ad in a magazine that offers a porn casting to become a "Porn Star".
In galley girls and money, the quest for what seems to be the best job then the world will turn into an initiatory journey and become the biggest adventure of their lives.
