After a car accident, a woman with a seemingly perfect life begins to have visions that suggest she is not who she thinks she is.
A woman named Alison is married to a successful doctor named Steve, and she is living what seems to be the perfect life. Then why does Steve make sure that Alison takes her pill every morning? Visions of a former life begin to plague Alison, and they involve a different husband and a young daughter. What is going on? Do not go to the Lifetime network website to find out, or they will give away the ending. Just watch and enjoy the unfolding mystery.
