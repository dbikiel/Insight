Elango and his friend Pulivetti Arumugam come in possession of a time machine and start profiting from it. However, their meddling with time prevents the death of a gangster, who begins to wreck havoc in their lives.
A wannabe entrepreneur Elango &amp; his fake astrologer friend, Pulivetti Arumugam are down on luck in their respective lives. After getting humiliated by his ladylove's father, the crestfallen Elango &amp; his friend chance upon a time-machine. Their adventures with the time-machine form the rest of the movie
