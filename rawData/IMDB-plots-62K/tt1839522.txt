The impact of a selfless deed of benevolence and heroism right in the middle of the raging Bosnian war inextricably intertwines the lives of five people who are still affected by its consequences, over a decade later.
Five people are affected by a tragic heroic act. 12 years later, each of them will have to confront the past, through their own crisis. Will they overcome the guilt, frustration, urge for revenge? Will they do the right thing, at all costs?
The impact of a selfless deed of benevolence back in 1993 in the small town of Trebinje right in the middle of the raging Bosnian war still echoes years later, as three diverse stories of men and their families are inextricably intertwined. As a result, in the present, in 2005, by a twist of fate and a string of dramatic and totally unforeseen incidents will be confirmed that the dead are by no means forgotten, as the lives of five people still pivot around that act of heroism. It's been already twelve years later, and all those directly affected by that dreadful episode battle with the consequences, in need to tie up the loose ends. The war may be over, however, not for everyone.
