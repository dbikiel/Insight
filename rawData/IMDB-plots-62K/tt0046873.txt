When Amadeu discovers that his uncle, Mr. Costa, is returning from the Portuguese colonies in Africa, he panics. He wasted all the money he gave him so he will try to create an illusion for his uncle not to realize that he's broke.
Living in Africa, Mr. Costa decides to drop by Lisbon for a few hours and see how his nephew Amadeu, who he has been financially supporting for years, is doing. Having squandered all of his uncle's investment, Amadeu passes off a friend's lavish home and lifestyles as his own. Troubles arise though as his uncle decides to stay quite a bit longer...
