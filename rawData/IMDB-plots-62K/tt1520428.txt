A shocking revelation comes with Frank and Peter's New York apartment, as a motley assortment of fellow tenants and menacing strangers decide to pay a visit. Before long, an elderly neighbour; a deceived husband; an intimidating drug dealer, and finally, a cryptic young woman, will come banging on their door, thirsting to settle their scores with the building's newest renters. But, what secrets stain the seemingly normal flat?
