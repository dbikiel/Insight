A deformed hunchback gets a second chance in life, but it is abruptly taken away when he accidentally kills a man.
Bongkok has lived all his life with scorn and hatred from the people in his village due to crimes committed by his father. Banished from the village, he undergoes a magical transformation which leads to him killing a man in a duel. His new life is then taken away from him and he must atone for his sins by worshiping the devil.
