Help from a friend to pull the wisdom teeth does not end as expected.
After an important wisdom teeth operation, poor Nigel with his puffy face and the stitches still hanging out of his mouth bumps into an old friend. Inevitably, Nigel's friend intrigued by the fine sutures asks if he can pull them out, however, what starts as a harmless and small request, will eventually drag the two friends into a downward spiral of unforeseen consequences.
