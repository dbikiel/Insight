It is the story of a sportsman who is a little bit dumber than most people but nevertheless he succeeds in getting a job at a sports article saler's. Having this job is the beginning of a lot of funny problems he causes due to his dumbness.
A lower class sportsman finds himself working for a highly known sports emporium, due to his charisma and personality he is sent to Acapulco to close a deal with a world famous athlete, in Acapulco he finds the love of his life... But there's one problem the woman he's in love with believing he is the world-famous athlete.
