A Gainesville Florida auto upholsterer (Giamatti) attempts to subvert his mundane life by training a wild, red-tailed hawk.
"The Hawk is Dying" stars Academy Award nominee Paul Giamatti (Sideways, The Illusionist, Cinderella Man) as a man who attempts to transcend his mundane life by taming a wild red-tailed hawk. He chases his passion while he cares for his nephew and becomes caught up in an abstract and uneasy relationship with a young psychology student played by Academy Award nominee Michelle Williams (Brokeback Mountain, Imaginary Heroes, The Station Agent)."
