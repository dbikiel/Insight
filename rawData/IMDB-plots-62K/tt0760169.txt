Something awaits Tyler Jensen and his friends in the Hallettsville house. When Tyler's lifelong friends start dying, he must figure out a way to save himself, his girlfriend and the rest of the crew before the demons come.
Gary Busey stars as a no-nonsense sheriff in HALLETSVILLE, an intense indie horror flick about terror in a small town. When a group of friends decide to spend one last weekend in a soon-to-be-sold ranch house, they wind up confronting more than just emotional demons. As the young adults begin to suffer sudden, mysterious deaths, the remaining friends must scramble to silence the horrific poltergeists.
