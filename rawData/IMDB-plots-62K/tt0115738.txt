An engineer finds his first gray hair, takes 6 days off from wife, son and work, rents a car and meets different people.
Al Fountain, a middle-aged electrical engineer, is on the verge of a mid-life crisis, when he decides to take his time coming home from a business trip, rents a car, and heads out looking for a lake he remembers from his childhood. But his wandering takes him into the life of Kid, a free-spirited young man who helps Al escape from the routine of everyday life and find freedom to enjoy himself.
Life sends Al fountain a sprite by the name of "The Kid" to help him take a break from his life. Al gets away long enough to appreciate the things he has. Lightweight, unlikely, fun story. Low-budget film that might make you think about the good things in your own life, or maybe just make you wish you could duck out of life for a few days. Either way, it should make you feel that it's ok to be Al.
