After a ten year absence, "El Chato" returns to San Ignacio, his hometown, in order to retrieve a shipment of cocaine that was dropped there by a cartel pilot.
Just before crashing, a drug cartel pilot drops a shipment of cocaine in San Ignacio, a small town in the middle of nowhere. The cartel's boss sends El Chato to retrieve it. Turns out that that San Ignacio is El Chato's hometown, a place he hasn't set foot in over ten years. Not only has El Chato got to figure out how to find and gather 500 packages of cocaine, but he's also got to face all the people he left behind.
