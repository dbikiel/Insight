Notorious smuggler and gangster named Singhania gets into a feud with another gangster named Dhaneshwar. Singhania gets Dhaneshwar framed for stealing foreign diamonds. Dhaneshwar, after his release from jail, along with the help of Tejeshwar, entraps Singhania and gets him killed with the help of law enforcement officers. Singhania's two sons Raj and Prem vow revenge. Following their plan, Prem enters the police force and starts raiding and decimating Dhaneshwar's empire. But soon he develops a conscience and vows to walk the path of honesty which creates a rift between the brothers.
