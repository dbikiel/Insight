Kunjiraman (Vineeth) lives in a remote, old world village in Kerala named Desam, surrounded by greenery, myths and legends.
Kunjiraman (Vineeth Sreenivasan) is a young Non-resident Indian who returns to his hometown Desam. He is planning to get married soon and is searching for a correct partner. His cousin brother Laalu (Dhyan Sreenivasan) secretly plots against Kunjiraman. They are brought together by Kuttan (Aju Varghese).
