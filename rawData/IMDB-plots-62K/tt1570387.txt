Louise's mother, Susan was in prison and that's why she lives with her gran. But now her mum has been released. She thinks her mum is just great. Seen through the eyes of this teenager, Susan is cool, decisive and absolutely irresistible. Desperate for her mother's love and recognition, she is quite content to party all night long at her mum's side. Under her wing, she is introduced to a completely new and exciting world; a constant round of parties, drugs, a fast buck - and men. But - wait a minute. She is only a teenager.
