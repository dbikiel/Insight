After her grandfather dies, a girl discovers a portal into another dimension.
12-year-old Riley Ann Taylor recently lost her grandfather, who died right in front of her while teaching her how to play soccer. She soon finds a portal leading to the place people talk about but never see: Heaven. She gains a miraculous healing ability and uses it to help those who are sick but soon gains the diseases these people and animals had. You know what they say, every good thing comes with a price.
Riley, a young girl who believes in Heaven, acquires the power to heal people and animals from sickness. Her grandfather's passage has been hard on the family. Can Riley mend her broken family through her belief in the spirit of Heaven?
A small town girl who discovers a window into Heaven and uses her new found powers to help heal and bolster faith in the small community until she is faced with the ultimate sacrifice. Jdb
