Indulge your stiff upper lip, British pride and self-deprecating awareness as mild mannered Miles takes you on a journey of middle class woes. Poking fun at his BBC Radio 4 listening, Waitrose shopping audience (as well completely surrendering to the fact he's one of them) there are endless laughs to be had from Mr. Jupp's inappropriately grandiose reaction to the slightest inconvenience.
