Four teenage friends go on a road trip through Argentina. On their way, they find a castle left abandoned in the middle of a forest. They decide to investigate further, only to discover it's gruesome secrets and former residents.
During the early seventies, four teenage friends go on a trip through the interior of Argentina in an attempt to watch an illegal car race take place in the streets of Cordoba. On their way, they discover a mysterious castle left abandoned in the middle of the forest. They decide to investigate further, just to discover it's gruesome secrets and the ruined house's former residents.
