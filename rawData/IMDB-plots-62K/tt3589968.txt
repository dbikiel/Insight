A surreptitious smuggler gets solicited by the CIA to help cover-up the Nicaraguan blackmail attempt on the CIA, after the release of "Dark Alliance", by Pulitzer Prize winning journalist, Gary Webb.
An events based drama showcasing the affairs of a surreptitious smuggler of black market merchandise, working for a former member of the German Stasi, along with his CIA solicited and deleterious involvement with Flight TWA 800 and his aided cover-up of the Nicaraguan blackmail attempt on the CIA, after the release of Dark Alliance, by Pulitzer Prize winning author, Gary Webb.
