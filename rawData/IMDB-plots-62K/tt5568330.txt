Mr. Naser Izadi and Mrs. Izadi were invited to Brazil by an agent from tourism agency. Angela (a Brazilian woman) misunderstood Naser as her ex-fiance and this make a challenging event for their trip.
Naser is a teacher , accidentally he found a bag full of money but he return it to the owner without any expectation. One travel agency prizes him and his family for a free trip to Brazil. Funny situations has been brought up by this matter.
Naser is on a holiday in Brazil with his family. He does not suspect yet but the family vacation that started out so peacefully is about to take a huge twist. First, he is taken by mistake as the former boyfriend of Angela, a local girl from a rich family. She gets him involved in a deal in order to earn her grandmother's inheritance who is also visiting Brazil. This creates an unstoppable flow of events making things only more complicated. Next to the beautiful setting, this light-hearted comedy offers a great time for the whole family.
