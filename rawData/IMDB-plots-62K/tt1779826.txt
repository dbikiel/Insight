Manolo's life is in crisis: his marriage is failing, his relationship with his two children is not great,and he hates his monotonous job. However, everything changes when Antonio, a 15-year-old cancer patient comes into his life in this heart-warming Christmas story.
Manolo (Diego Peretti) is in mid-life crisis. His marriage with Beatriz (Aitana Sánchez-Gijón) is on the verge of chaos, the routine of his work is unbearable and communication with his two children and with life itself is disastrous. However, fate leads him to come across Antonio (Andoni Hernández), a 15-year-old Canarian boy with cancer but with a very contagious vitality. Since then, his friendship with him, his mother (Goya Toledo), a fun food delivery (Jorge Garcia) and an extravagant nurse (Rosa María Sardà), among others, will be the beginning of a myriad of coincidences that will change his Life in a fun and magical fascinating Christmas tale.
