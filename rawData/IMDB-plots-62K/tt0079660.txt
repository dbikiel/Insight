When her marriage falls apart, a psychiatrist seeks out those of her former boyfriends who left the biggest impact on her life, mostly in a bad way.
John Belushi was the high school sweetheart who ruined her reputation with lies. Richard Jordan was the college lover who she almost married. Keith Carradine is the unstable brother of her first real love. Talia Shire is Diane Cruise, a woman whose life has been shattered in a myriad pieces. After the dissolution of her marriage, she decides to delve into her past and seek out the men who marked the milestones in her life.
