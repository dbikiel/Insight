Jocelyn is a successful businessman, selfish and misogynist. He tries to seduce a young pretty woman by pretending to be handicapped, till the day she presents him her sister in a wheelchair.
A successful businessman, who is flirtatious and a compulsive liar by nature, pretends to be confined to a wheelchair in an artful bid to seduce a young woman. His clever ruse goes pear-shaped when he is introduced to his fiance's sister who happens to be in a wheelchair too.
