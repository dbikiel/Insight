Aliens visit the solar-powered house of a middle-class family, and the house is suddenly sucked into a time warp that takes it to a planet with prehistoric looking creatures, a Bermuda Triangle dumping ground and finally to another planet 200 light years from earth in a triple star system inhabited by an advanced civilization. The family stays there never to return to earth.
