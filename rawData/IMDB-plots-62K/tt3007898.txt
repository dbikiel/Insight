An odd couple proves that love goes deeper than just flesh and blood.
At first glance, Margie and Barry may seem as a discordant, almost odd couple, after all, the age gap in their relationship is far too big to remain unnoticed. However, regardless of what most people might think, this is a truly loving couple; Barry still cares for Margie's every need with an unconditional and selfless devotion, that after all these years, is simply inspiring. In the end, it's all those fine and noble details that make us human.
