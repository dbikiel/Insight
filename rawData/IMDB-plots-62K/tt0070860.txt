Uuno Turhapuro is a man who can convince anyone of anything by simply talking them into submission. Uuno learns to play the violin and becomes famous, but discovers that fame is both a gift and a curse.
Uuno Turhapuro is a man with a talent, the gift of the gab. He can acccomplish, or get away with, anything as long as someone is willing to listen to his reasons and explanations. And he uses his gift often, mostly on his long-suffering wife. In this, the character's debut film, Uuno learns to play the violin and becomes famous, but discovers that fame is both a gift and a curse.
