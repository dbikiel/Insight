Jussi Vares goes in a small town to investigate a murder of young girl. This little goodloving town isn't as pure as it seems.
In a deserted area in Finland, the corpse of a young woman is found.
The finder, a former pastor, hires Jussi Vares to investigate the crime.
It is the beginning of the most bizarre case of Vares' career.
He travels to Kahdenvirranmaa, a poor village in the grip of a powerful sect.
