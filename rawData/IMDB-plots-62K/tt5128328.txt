A wannabe rowdy who falls for a hearing-impaired girl faces a dilemma when the girl makes a shocking request.
Pandi, son of a lady inspector in Pondicherry is a wannabe rowdy whose life-time ambition is to become a fearsome don but is an extremely fearful &amp; jovial lad otherwise. He falls for the hearing impaired Kadhambari and tries to accomplish her request to take on the dreaded don, Killivalavan.
