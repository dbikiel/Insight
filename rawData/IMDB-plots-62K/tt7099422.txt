'Rock' - a music genre or just plain bad luck? The heroes of the film, who decided to conquer the musical Olympus of Moscow are followed by both. This trio is not a band or a team and, probably, are not even friends yet. But this is only the beginning of their journey. The farther the heroes go away from home, the more dangerous are their adventures and the people encountered on the way, the more the guys are changing internally. The film is an adventure and a search for oneself, where the road to a dream became the biggest, brightest, most dangerous and unforgettable adventure of a lifetime for the young provincial musicians.
