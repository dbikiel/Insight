A teenager and her friends become trapped in a haunted theater with a disturbing connection to Amityville's secret past.
Following the tragic death of her parents Fawn Harriman discovers she has inherited a theatre in the town of Amityville. She, along with 3 friends, decides to spend the weekend there looking the place over. Meanwhile one of her High School teachers begins an investigation into the village's past and makes a connection with something that goes back beyond recorded history.
After the untimely death of her parents, Fawn Harriman inherits a dilapidated theatre in the legendary town of Amityville. However, instead of selling the decrepit property, Fawn decides to spend the night in the run-down building along with her friends to give the old place a look-see, only to come face-to-face with mysterious supernatural occurrences and a scary homeless woman. As Fawn's high-school teacher starts investigating the theatre's haunted history, a strange connection to Amityville's dark past becomes evident. Now, the unsuspecting visitors find themselves trapped in this evil theatre. Is there a way out from this black portal of hell?
