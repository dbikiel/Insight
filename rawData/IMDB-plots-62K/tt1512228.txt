An ambitious young filmmaker discovers an abandoned and incomplete horror movie from the 1980s and decides to finish it - big mistake.
An ambitious young filmmaker named James finds a horror film named The Streetwalker that was left uncompleted when the original director committed suicide. Unfortunately, his efforts appear to be doomed from the start, with every type of problem effecting the production. Over time, James begins to develop an obsession that the first filming may have been more real than he expected. As he spins more and more out of control, his friend records his progress.
