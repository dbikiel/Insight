Crippled by his writer's block, Paul enters into a new, exciting relationship with risk-taking Billy and super-sexy Juliette. As it becomes increasingly tangled, however, he must choose one of them over the other.
Paul, a struggling artist starts a relationship with Juliette. The same day they meet, he makes the acquaintance of Billy and Hole whose lives are a long string of drugs, sex, and violence. He veers between life with Juliette and her son, and the debauched excesses of Billy and Hole.
