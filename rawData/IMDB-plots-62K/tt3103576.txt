A literature professor at the University of Lausanne, Marc has a reputation for having love affairs with his female students. A few days after the disappearance of one of the most brillant of these, who was his latest conquest, he encounters Anna who is trying to find out more about this beautiful young woman who has disappeared.
After a brief clandestine affair with her forty-something Literature professor and serial seducer, Marc, the beautiful creative-writing student, Barbara, mysteriously disappears. However--even though Marc openly admits he was with Barbara the night before--he seems unworried, and to further complicate matters, the police and the missing student's alluring stepmother, Anna, start asking questions. As friends and investigators alike gradually tighten the noose on the cryptic tutor, much to Marc's surprise, he begins to experience a long-forgotten feeling: a genuine passion for only one woman. Is love the perfect crime?
