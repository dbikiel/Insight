A high-end car dealership owner is forced to deal with the Finnish mafia over unsatisfactory cars his dead partner sold them.
Maxime has everything he needs to be happy: manager of an up-and-coming high-end car dealership, he's married to a beautiful young woman and lives in a comfortable villa. Everything changes the day Simon, his partner, is murdered by a group of thugs. Forced to deal with them himself, Maxime asks his brothers for help as they are used to small-time felonies. But their interference is a fiasco, and Maxime and his family's situation becomes more and more inextricable...
