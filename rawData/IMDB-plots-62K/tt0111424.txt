Guan Jian wants to report the murder of his father who died 10 years ago. The alleged murderer whom Guan Jian accuses of the crime is his own mother.
Based on real events in China 1988, a son (Chung-Hua Tao) has to come to terms with the implications of informing that his mother (Gaowa Siqin) may have murdered his father ten years ago to police, a crime that will undoubtedly carry the death sentence.
