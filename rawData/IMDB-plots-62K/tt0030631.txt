Egyptologist, Dean Lambert (Lloyd), accused of car-theft, skips bail and begins a cross-country trek to join a group in New York headed for Egypt. With the police close on his trail he gets in and out of scrapes along the way.
Respected Egyptologist Dean Lambert has his scholarly life upset when he begins to fall for a strange woman who keeps turning up. He is frightened by parallels he notices between his life and that of an Egyptian man he has studied, who was killed as a result of a forbidden love that he had for a similar woman 3000 years ago. Can Professor Lambert escape that fate?
