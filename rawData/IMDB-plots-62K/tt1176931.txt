"Las Buenas Hierbas". Daughter of a herb healer woman dealing with illness try to found empathy with each other.
Dalias life changes when she must face Alzheimer's, the disease that attacks her mother Lola, an etno-botanist. This is a story about brain and plant chemistry and the human emotions. The invisible ties linking living beings to their dead and to nature, to which we all belong. It is about Mexican herbalism which shows us some plants that can help to heal the soul.
