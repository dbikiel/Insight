Legendary radio personality Bob Fass revolutionized late night FM radio by serving as a cultural hub for music, politics and audience participation for nearly 50 years. Long before todays innovations in social media, Fass utilized the airwaves for mobilization encouraging luminaries and ordinary listeners to talk openly and take the program in surprising directions. RADIO UNNAMEABLE is a visual and aural collage that pulls from Bob Fasss immense archive of audio from his program, film, photographs, and video that has been sitting dormant until now.
