Saturday night in Berlin; a bunch of friends go out to have fun. Others are also out exploring the city and their relationships. A colorful mix of singles, couples, heteros, homosexuals, poly-amorous. Different personalities, for different reasons, give a spectrum of contemporary approaches to nightlife. They go all the way, exceeding their limits. Their journey will lead them into the heart of a frenetic night where everything is allowed. But everything here is rather comical and our characters end up where they do due to a series of mishaps.
