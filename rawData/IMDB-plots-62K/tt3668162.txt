Three cousins set out to fulfill their Bangalore dreams.
Bangalore Days begins with an intro from Kuttan (Nivin Pauly) introducing himself and his cousins namely Divya (Nazriya) and Aju (Dulquer) who share a very friendly relationship right from the childhood.They get separated after their schooling but are reunited at Bangalore after the marriage of Divya to Das. The story revolves around how their life turns at Bangalore.
Arjun (Dulquer Salmaan), Kuttan (Nivin Pauly), and Divya (Nazriya Nazim) are cousins, who arrive at Bangalore for various reasons. Arjun is a bike mechanic by profession and lives life on his own terms.He meet a girl RJ Sarah (Parvathy) and Fall in Love with her. Kuttan is a typical Archie. He Wants to marry a village girl in Bangalore. Divya is married to Das (Fahadh Faasil), who is a high-flying corporate executive. Their marriage life is not in good terms, as Das turns out to be a workaholic. In this course, Divya comes to know about Das's old love story (with Natasha (Nithya Menon)), which makes her stay away from him.
