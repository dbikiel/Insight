Two years ago, the iconic metal band, Lamb of God, set out to make a movie about how their genre of music has gone global. Midway through filming, lead singer Randy Blythe was arrested and charged with manslaughter in the Czech republic. This caused the project, which was originally meant for fans, to drastically switch tracks, turning it into an edge-of-your-seat legal thriller. This first look at As the Palaces Burns offers insight into the band's life during this time, and shows how they dealt with the whole ordeal.
