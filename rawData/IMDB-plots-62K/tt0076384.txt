The main character is unhappily married and has an affair. When his wife finds out, she lures his girlfriend to a cliff and throws herself of it, making the girlfriend look as her killer. The main character then tries to draw suspicion from his girlfriend to himself. It appears he wil succeed...
Henri Savin leaves his mistress Dominique Montlaur for another woman, young and beautiful Julie Manet. After a confrontation with Julie, Dominique jumps off a cliff and dies. Inspector Waldeck concludes that she was murdered by Julie. Then Savin comes up with an elaborate plan to save his lover.
