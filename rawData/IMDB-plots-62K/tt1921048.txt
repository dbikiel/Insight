The journey of a young mother in a prison with her baby boy.
Ombline, a young woman of twenty, was sentenced to three years in prison after a violent assault. While she has lost all hope in the future, an event disrupts her life: she discovers she is pregnant and gives birth to Lucas. The law allowing him to raise the first 18 months, Ombline will fight to keep her son as long as possible with her and convince the judge that she is able to take care his release from prison. In this dark prison world, begins the struggle of a woman who became a mother in prison, which will be rebuilt by fighting for her child.
