How do perfectly ordinary, normal people cope with the extraordinary challenge of an embarrassing, provocative, famous or unbelievable name? This documentary examines the phenomena of "strange names."
We've all known the kid who lived in fear of morning roll call at school. The teenaged girl assailed with cruel nicknames and rude limericks. The man who dreads giving his name to the maitre'd. We've gathered them together to share their stories -- the funny, the tragic, and everything in between.
