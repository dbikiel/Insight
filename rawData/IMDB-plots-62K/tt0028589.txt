Joan Barry stands to inherit her late grandmother's fortune if she is able to fulfill a morals clause in the will. However, her greedy cousin Linda and her husband Jack plan to frame Joan by getting her loaded on marijuana and getting caught with Jack in a motel room. It is up to cub reporter Art Brighton to not only save Joan, but to bust a gang of dope peddlers, which Linda Belongs to, infecting the town.
