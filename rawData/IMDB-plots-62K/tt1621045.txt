Four friends conspire to turn the tables on their women when they discover the ladies have been using Steve Harvey's relationship advice against them.
Four women have given up on getting their men to do what they want until they find a how to book written by Steve Harvey. They start using his advice about relationships, and their men start falling in line, until the men discover what the women have been doing, then the war is on.
Four interconnected and diverse men whose love lives are shaken up after the ladies they are pursuing buy Steve Harveys book 'Think Like a Man' and start taking his advice to heart. When the band of brothers realize they have been betrayed by one of their own, they conspire using the books insider information to turn the tables and teach the women a lesson of their own.
