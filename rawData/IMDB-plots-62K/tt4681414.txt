A pretty young girl and a sweet but plain-looking boy fall in love. Will their relationship prevail?
Gouda Takeo is a big guy with a heart just as big. Unfortunately, the girls are not interested in him and only want his good-looking best friend, Sunakawa. Accepting the fact, Takeo is content with his single life. However, one day, after saving a girl named Yamato from a harasser, he unexpectedly turns his life around.
