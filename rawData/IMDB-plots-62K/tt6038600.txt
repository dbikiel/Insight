Inspired by true events of 2010 Polish Air Force Tu-154 mysterious crash in Smolensk, the film tells the fictional story of various people affected by the tragedy. The protagonist is a journalist Nina, who refuses to accept the official version of the story and pursues her own independent investigation.
Polish Air Force One arriving from Warsaw with current administration and military command, to attend an event commemorating the 70th anniversary of the Katyn massacre, disintegrates in unexplained way over Smolensk. What is worth noting, is the fact, that reconstruction of the crash is based on witness testimonies. "This is a movie, with the fact that everything that is directly related to the disaster, we based on the facts - said director Antoni Krauze at the premiere of "Smolensk".
