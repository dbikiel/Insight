It chronicles the love relationship between a boy called Motoharu Yano and a girl called Nanami Takahashi, starting from their teenage years and continuing during their early twenties.
Sometimes love is impossible. A girl and a boy from Hokkaido in Northern Japan fall in love and pledge eternal fealty to each other. Circumstances soon separate them geographically, but the two are no less in love and soldier on with one another. Soon, however, it becomes clear that loving someone is more than maintaining a linear path and abiding by one's promise. Well, at least it becomes clear to one of the two.
We Were There follows the 10 year romance between Motoharu Yano (Toma Ikuta) &amp; Nanami Takahashi (Yuriko Yoshitaka). During their high school days, Motoharu Yano was the most popular boy in school, but cant get over the death of his girlfriend from a car crash. Nanami Takahashi falls in love with Motoharu Yano and must deals with his inability to let go of the past.
