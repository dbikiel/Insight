Nico d'Altamura is a shy sixteen-year-old boy. During the summer, he becomes acquainted with his cousin Sonia, who stays at his home for some time. She grew up in the city and is much more open and uninhibited than her cousin. An equivocal relationship between them starts to develop, which disrupts Nico's life. At the end of the summer, before leaving for Rome, Sonia finds a way of sharing her bed with him.
