A young girl accustomed to living luxuriously is relegated to servitude at a boarding school when her rich father is suddenly broke and dies in far-off India.
Sara Crewe is the pampered darling of her army-captain father and the Victorian London girls' school where he places her. But when her father dies penniless, Sara becomes a skivvy in Miss Michin's school, befriended only by the scullery maid Becky, her friends Ermengarde and Lottie, a little monkey, a Lascar, and the mysterious man next door.
