A cruel dictator rules a Latin American state. Corruption, brutality and exploitation are present every day. A few people begin to organise resistance. Under the leader "El Leopardo" a small group of guerillas fights against the violent government...
Under constant attack by hostile dictatorial power in a country of South America, where the rules power, powerlessness and violence reigns, the revolutionary army under the command of the famous 'Carrasco', try to save the helpless people of violence to overthrow a dictator.
