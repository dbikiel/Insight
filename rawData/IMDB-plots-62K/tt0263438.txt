When Altan swipes prescription drugs from his brother Nuri's pharmacy, they soon find themselves on a dangerous but funny road trip to get rid of the stuff and escape the mafiosi Altan tried to double-cross. Along the way, the brother who are compete opposites finally bond.
After a few years,Altan and Nuri brothers encounter.Altan is an unemployed man and obsessed the idea of opening a bar.Nuri works at a medicine store.Altan comes to Nuri's medicine store and he secretly steals prescriptive drugs.After a while brothers go to Bodrum where Altan sells the drugs but Nuri isn't aware of anything.They have an amazing and funny time at Bodrum.They really become closer after a long time.However when they return to Istanbul they find themselves in a complicated and dangerous situation but they never lose hope because 'Everything's Gonna Be Great'.
