A VeggieTales of Queen Esther and her struggle to save her people from being sent to the Island of Perpetual Tickling (IPT) by her husband the king.
Esther has been chosen by the king to be queen, but she's about to find out that being queen is going to take more courage than she ever imagined! Esther... The Girl Who Became Queen teaches kids that "you never need to be afraid to do what's right!"
