A story about a broken-hearted girl who meets a boy in a not so normal way. Together, they go to places and find out "where do broken hearts go?".
The films asks the questions "Where do broken hearts go?" and tells the story of a woman struggling to meet airline baggage requirements who meets a man who comes to her aid. Both in despair out of love, they form a charming friendship that will take them to Sagada in their attempts to mend each other's hearts.
Devastated with her recent breakup, Mace has no choice but to go to her homecountry and move on. With a help of a newfound friend, she went to random places in hopes of finding comfort. Where will Mace's broken heart take her? Will it be towards recovery? Heartache? Or another romance?
