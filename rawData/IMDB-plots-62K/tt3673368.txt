Sudden and strong cooling has spread around the world. Harsh blizzards and low temperatures caught the people unprepared. Cities were leveled and countries were demolished. Every form of government ceased to exist. Some survivors are trying to reach Africa where the cooling is milder. Desolate cities are being ravaged by looters and raiders, showing no mercy for anyone. Three friends, Michael, Victor and Alex must face their fears in order to survive in a world ruled by cruel reality.
