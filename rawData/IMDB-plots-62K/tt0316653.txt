A wily businessman plots with a sultry executive to swindle $40 million from his father. But who is conning who?
Lauren Bishop, an efficient executive, meets Clay Harding, the son of the owner of the company where she works, in a business meeting. After having intense sexual intercourse, they decide to deviate the company money to a Swiss bank. Last, a cheating game between them two will make a winner and a looser.
Lauren Bishop is a financial analyst at a large investment banking firm. Her new playmate, Clay Harding, is the son of the very wealthy owner of a client firm. They develop a complicated scheme to allow Clay to claim his inheritance without waiting for Dad's passing away.
