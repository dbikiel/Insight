A struggling musician moves in with his gay best friend in order to restart his life only to find himself falling in love with their lesbian roommate.
Brad, after being evicted from his apartment is convinced to move in with his gay best friend Emile. Their friendship is tested for the first time when Brad begins to fall for their other housemate, Michelle, a dancer who happens to be gay also. His relationship with Michelle develops beyond anything either of them ever foresaw and produces complications with Brad and Emile's friendship. Brad must decide what is best for everyone as hearts start to break and friendships are torn.
