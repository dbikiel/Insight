A womaniser, the titular big mouth, helps his introvert best friend find true love.
Claudy Faucan is manager of a slaughterhouse in Belgium and an amateur photographer.
After a photoshoot with beautiful Natacha, an extrovert sexy woman, he embarks on a journey of strange encounters.
JC and Stef are inseparable friends but complete opposites. JC is a ladykiller, THE dikkenek, whilst Stef is shy and secretive.
Nadine, rich kid and teacher who cares more about drugs and fun than about her pupils... and Fabienne, a crazy police superintendent who's not good with grammar and plays too valiantly with her gun.
