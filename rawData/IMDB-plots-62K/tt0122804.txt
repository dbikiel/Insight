Eight friends in Los Angeles spend their last evening together as they face graduation from high school and the onset of their adult lives. One of them gets in unexpected trouble when he loses a large sum of his dad's money. The friends rally together to attempt to raise the money back in one evening in a wild and desperate scavenger hunt.
This comedy-drama centers around eight Asian-American teenagers in Los Angeles on their last night together before high school graduation. On that evening, Sin Lee, one of the teens, tells his friends that he lost $1500 of his dad's money while getting held-up in his father's Korean grocery. Sin fears that once his parents discover the money is missing, he'll be forced to work in his dad's store instead of going off to college. The guys and girls rally together to help Sin recover the money.
