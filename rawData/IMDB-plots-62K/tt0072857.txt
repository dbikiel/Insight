On March 13, 1964, Catherine "Kitty" Genovese was attacked and viciously stabbed to death several times by a psychopath while nearby residents watched but did nothing to help.
A young woman is murdered in an alley. The crime is heard or seen by the residents of a nearby apartment building, but none of them did anything to help, and they refused to cooperate with the police during the investigation.
Based on a true story, a young woman is stabbed to death in an alley while several people witness it. Because of their inaction, the killer got away and began a wave of terror in which one woman was almost raped, and another woman was raped and killed. Can the police stop him before he strikes again?
