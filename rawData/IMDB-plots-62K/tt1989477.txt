Children of the Air is a story of fleeting love. Past and present bleed into one as we follow the rise and demise of a young couple's fairytale love affair. Juxtaposed to the original fairytale of the Little Mermaid, Children of the Air is an unflinching portrait of heartbreak. Katheryn Winnick and Travis Van Winkle star as Samantha and Trey, a young couple trying to salvage a haunting moment that has passed them by. Winnick and Van Winkle relive the wonder and disappointment of fleeting love and broken moments left behind.
