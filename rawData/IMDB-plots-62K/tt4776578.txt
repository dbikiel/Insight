Charles Dickens' classic tale of love, loss and hope comes to life in an all new original musical featuring Colin Baker (Doctor Who). When the miserly Ebenezer Scrooge (Anthony D.P. Mann) receives ghostly visitations one fateful Christmas Eve, he is shown the error of his ways. This inspirational new adaptation is brimming with holiday warmth and music that will ensure its place as a new tradition for families and viewers of all ages.
