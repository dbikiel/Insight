Luke hates starting middle school and wishes for the last day of summer, the festival, to keep repeating itself. It does, at least 100 times, until he's learned his lesson.
The fun starts when 11-year old Luke wishes he could have his last day of vacation all over again - and his dreams come true! But when he relives the last day of summer over and over again, he soon finds himself eager for life to continue, whatever the future holds!
Luke Malloy (Jansen Panettiere) wished every day could be the last day of summer vacation. He soon loses consciousness and wakes up to find himself living the same day again and again. To make it to tomorrow, he must overcome every obstacle that comes his way.
