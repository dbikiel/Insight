France under occupation by the Germans during WW2: Two men inspired by a free France radio broadcast perpetrate an act of sabotage with unwitting consequences for them and their fellow villagers.
Pouzay, a school teacher performs as a clown in a show. His son is obviously ashamed of his dad. Designy, his best friend decides to tell the boy how his father became a clown. A story of world war II, where a humane act of a person marks the life of others for ever.
