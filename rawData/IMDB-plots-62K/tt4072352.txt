Magallanes is a veteran soldier who struggles to survive driving an irregular taxi cab around the poorest neighborhoods in Lima. His loyalty to his former superior officers and their goodwill to him are what has kept his everyday life in balance. That's until a girl from the past takes a drive in his cab and unbeknownst to her, lights up a forgotten fire in his soul.
