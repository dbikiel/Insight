The brainless teenagers form Brain Drain are back and this time they will be going to the Veterinary Faculty at Harvard University.
Alfonso, the younger brother of Emilio Carbajosa, blood blood of the protagonist of the previous game in the series, pursues the girl of his life to Harvard, determined to win under the same friends and the same harebrained schemes his brother used in Oxford. But when you least expect it, appears Marta, the girl who fell in love when they were children.
