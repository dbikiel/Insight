France nineteenth century : Two middle aged orphans Gaspare and Battista find out (thanks to a soothsayer) that one is the offspring of the Hangman of Paris and the other is the son of a count. But it is only a dream of theirs...
In Paris, in 1865, in an orphan-asylun everyone is an orphan. The girls, of course, the headmistress, the dog and the two watchmen, Gasparre and Battista: each of them is an orphan. Gasparre and Battista decide to look for an orphan's father so she can marry. They go to a chiromancer to seek advise and Gasparre finds out he is the son of a count. They found his relatives and they get involved in a series af adventures and they risk their's skin.
