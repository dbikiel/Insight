The working-class hero of Chelsea Lately's Roundtable is all dressed up for an all-new 70 minutes of adorable profanity! John Caparulo has the stage all to himself at The South Point Hotel &amp; Casino in Las Vegas to give comedy fans a whole new side, the INSIDE!! Even more honest, entertaining, and hilarious than ever before, Cap invites everyone to come inside his world as he sees it, trading in his bachelorhood and T-shirts for a wedding band and buttons in this, the 2nd full-length stand-up comedy special from one of the best comedians in America today.
