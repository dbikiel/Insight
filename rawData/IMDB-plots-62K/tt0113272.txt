A young girl experiences disturbing visions of a tragedy that took place 30 years earlier, that may be connected to a recent unsolved murder.
A young and innocent girl named Lisa receives mysterious visions about the recent murder of a young child. When the visions become increasingly more intense, Lisa's mother asks for the help of a parapsychologist. Soon, these visions set in motion a search for the killer, who may also be responsible for a murder that occurred 30 years earlier.
