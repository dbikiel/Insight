A guy decides to sell weed after losing his job, but things soon go out of hand as demand of business and paranoia starts taking over.
A recent college graduate (Brener) decides to sell marijuana on the streets of Manhattan after losing his job at a consulting firm. He soon meets the girl of his dreams (Daddario). With an unsupportive girlfriend, an increase of clienteles, and the growing threats of being caught or killed, he soon realizes he is in way over his head.
Recently graduated from University, David, a timid 22-year-old aspiring novelist who works for a consultant in Midtown Manhattan, is trapped in a tedious job. Living only for the weekend, riding his bike and taking hallucinogens with his friends, unexpectedly, David meets with Kate at a party and his life changes. From a single man, David now hangs out with the most beautiful girl in the world who is really fond of him, likes spending time together, and on top of that, is willing to move in with him to redecorate not only his flat but his life as well. However, David's bubble will burst when all of a sudden, he will lose his job and just before his life hits rock bottom, he will decide to sell weed over the internet. With no clue about dealing, yet, inconspicuous and determined to succeed, David, before long, he will see his little business flourish, nevertheless, at the cost of losing his friends. Eventually, with his life gradually getting worse, will David find the courage to make a choice between Kate and his business before he is left all alone and things get out of hand?
After being let go from his job at a consulting firm, a bright eyed 22-year-old college graduate decides to enter the world of low level weed dealing on the streets of Manhattan. With a conflicted live-in girlfriend, an expanding clientele and the growing threat of getting caught or killed, he soon realizes he may be way in over his head.
