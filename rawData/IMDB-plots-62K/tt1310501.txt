A struggling artist Hee-do is offered a million dollars to bet his life if he loses a game against a rich old man named No-shik who is dying from a terminal disease. However if Hee Do loses the game, he must trade his youth to the old man..
One day Hee Do receives an anonymous phone call followed by a visit from a wealthy middle aged woman Hye Rin .Hee Do is whisked into a big mansion where he meets an old financial tycoon who challenges him to a devious little game. The catch is if Hee Do loses the game, he must trade his youth to the old man..
