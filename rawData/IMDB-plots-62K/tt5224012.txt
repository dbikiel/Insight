A romantic drama based on the story of Carley Allison, a promising 18 year old figure skater and singer who made medical history in her fight against a rare 1 in 3.5 billion type of sarcoma.
A truly touching story consisting of a smart, talented girl whose dreams come to a stop because it is discovered she has a rare form of throat cancer. Her family and her boyfriend John help her through every step of the way as she keeps her hopes up and continues to smile.
A promising young figure skater and singer (Sarah Fisher) is diagnosed with a rare form of cancer, but continues to follow her dreams and inspire others with her positive attitude. Sean Cisterna directed this biopic, which is based on the story of Canadian teen Carley Allison.
