A romantic comedy that tells the story of Gonzalo, a Spaniard, and Valentina, an Argentinian. They live on Margarita Island and manage a hotel that only admits couples. After several years together, their love is going through a crisis, maybe because of the exhaustion that time might cause, maybe because of the lack of passion. The arrival of new guests, among whom is the first single person staying at the hotel since they opened it, will put their relationship to the test. The main ingredients of this story are: the Caribbean Sea, jealousy, lies, and half-truths.
