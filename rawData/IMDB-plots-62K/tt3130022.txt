Alison is left home alone on Halloween night, but while chatting with her best friend Jenna over Skype, something terrifying happens to Jenna, leaving Alison helpless on the other side of the computer screen, watching in horror.
Freshly dumped by her foolish boyfriend, the lonely and sulky Alison spends the night of Halloween home alone in front of the screen of her laptop. In the background of a deranged convict's escape, Alison is having a conversation over Skype with her best friend, Jenna, when all of a sudden, a blurry shape in the thick shadows brings shivers down the spine. A quick investigation will soon be the prelude to a night of pure horror. Has the deranged killer stained his hands with Jenna's innocent blood?
