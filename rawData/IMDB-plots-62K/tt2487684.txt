A child's horrific murder brings to light the haunted truth of an affair between her mother and a neighbor. As a magazine reporter investigates the case, he finds that nothing is as it seems.
When a sensational crime is reported and the mother is accused and suspected the press is beating down doors to find out more. The investigation takes a different turn when facts and backgrounds of everyone is examined and more revealed. Nothing is what it seems and no one is as they come. This is a lesson two reporters are going to learn soon enough.
