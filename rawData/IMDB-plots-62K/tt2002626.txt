Nanami and Yano were in-love. But because of unavoidable circumstances, they cannot commit to their plans and promises. They can decided whether they want to live life as it is, or find a way back to each other's arms.
It is the sophomore year of high school and Yano has to transfer to a school in Tokyo because of her family. Yano promises Nanami that they will meet again. A couple of years pass and Nanami is searching for a job. Yano and Nanami have stayed in contact, but that soon changes.
