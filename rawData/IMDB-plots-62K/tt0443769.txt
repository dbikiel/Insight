A young boy travels back to the 17th century to battle pirates off the coast of Peru.
Alberto, a peruvian kid, go on a school trip to a fort called El Real Felipe in Callao, in there Alberto gets lost and enters into a portal that sends him to the 17th century. He discovers that Callao is going to ba attacked by a group of dutch pirates led by Jacques L'Hermite who wants to steal the gold of the King and conquer Callao. Will Alberto be able to do something to stop the pirates?
