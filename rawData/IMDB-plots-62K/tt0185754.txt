As a young hero goes to battle with flying dinosaurs, he also must face his own internal monsters as well.
This is a story about Pedring. A young boy who, to forget his longing for his american father. And to avoid and escape from his abusive stepfather. Finds comfort in the wilderness with his Aeta Friends. He soon discovers an egg that was spewed out of Mount Pinatubo's Eurption. The egg soon hatches a baby creatures and soon enough, grows into a giant pterodacty-like and becomes pedring's best friend, which he names it Bulkan. Together they'll go through exciting adventures, Helping and defending people in need.
