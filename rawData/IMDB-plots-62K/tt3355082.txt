When a woman's foot is found by the sea in Brest, Parisian Alma Schneider launches an investigation with forensic scientist César Istria. After discovery of the rest of the body, it is clear that the victim was actually a man.
Centers on an investigation after a woman's foot is found close to the sea on the coast. Police officer, Alma Schneider, a newcomer to the town, decides to delve deeper into the case, when no one seems to be interested in discovering the truth. To unfold this mystery she can only count on the help of Cesar Istria, an unconventional coroner. Alma and César unite forces in making sense of this succession of mysteries that lead them into the entrails of Brest, discovering the city's night life.
