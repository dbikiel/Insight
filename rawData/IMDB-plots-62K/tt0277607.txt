After being repeatedly denied her requests for a vacation by her sexist boss, a young woman finally explodes.
Only goal of honest Barica, which seem to her like a dream, is to go to deserved, but long time not used vacation in order to take her ill daughter to seaside to heal her bronchitis. What should she do when employer doesn't take his word, when satisfaction of his earthly pleasures and small interests sets above employeee's needs of life importance? Barica asked that question herself when between her and her goal interposed new colleague Jadranka. Person in such circumstances have two choices: to work further enduring injustice, or to fight against it, but queastion is how if legal system doesn't work, or take things in her own hands...
