Neznaika acquaint with the fundamentals of the capitalist system on the Moon and communicate with antisocial elements. His friend Ponchik is becoming very rich selling salt.
They are neither adults nor kids - somewhere in between, living in their separate boys and girls cozy dorms and doing whatever they love to do - inventors, artists, poets, mechanists, scientists and....just dreamers! These enthusiastic and creative folks enjoy their half-adult, half-kiddish lifestyles and entertain each other with the different tricks and practical jokes. The main hero stands out of the crowd - while the others try to live the normal life socializing as they can, he can never be rested, always coming up with the different unpredictable and unexpectable moves, which always make viewers burst with laugh....
