We're Still Here chronicles the fiftieth anniversary recording of Cash's controversial concept record Bitter Tears: Ballads of the American Indian. Fifty years later with the country once again struggling with social justice issues, artists such as Steve Earle, Emmylou Harris, Kris Kristofferson, and more came together under the direction of Grammy Award-winning producer Joe Henry to pay their own tribute to Cash, these extraordinary songs, and Native Americans
