A holiday wedding brings no joy to the bride's cousin, who was literally left by the groom right at the altar. But she might find her true love there when she gains a fake fiancé in the process.
Aspiring interior designer Layla O'Reilly is dreading the Christmastime wedding of her cousin who's marrying Layla's ex-fiancé. Although Seth Murphy is the one responsible for introducing the bride and groom, she begrudgingly accepts his offer to be her date as she's out of options. Seth gets carried away at the reception and announces they're engaged, forcing a mortified Layla to keep up the pretense. But as the faux couple spends more time together they find themselves feeling something very real.
Seth Murphy - Daniel Lissing is an old friend of Layla O'Reilly's (Jessica Lowndes) and they reunite at a party, Seth introduces Layla's Cousin to Layla's Fiancé, but they end up having feelings for each other. A few years later, Seth and Layla run into each other, Seth asks Layla if she would be his date to the wedding, and after a while of convincing Layla says 'yes'. At the reception, Seth and Layla had an idea to be a fake couple, Seth, however, takes it too far and says they're engaged. Layla gets angry. Later Layla finds out the Stanley Malcolm sold Seth his house and is being used for the holiday tour of homes and Seth hires Layla to decorate it, they grew closer and closer as the tour date arrived. They each grow feelings for each other after that, and during an engagement surprise party their families throw for them, something happens for the better.
