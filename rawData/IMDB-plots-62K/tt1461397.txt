On Christmas Eve, two sisters sharing a Venice apartment discover their home is the final target of a brutal gang of criminals led by Jess and his sadistic sister Lorraine.
On Christmas Eve, Abbie and her wheelchair-bound sister Michele are forced to put aside their personal differences in order to survive an all-night onslaught of violence when they discover their Venice loft complex is the target of a group of vicious home invaders led by Jess and his sadistic sister Lorraine.
