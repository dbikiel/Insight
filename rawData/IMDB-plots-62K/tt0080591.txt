Ana Pozdnjakova wants to have loving husband, kids and family happiness. She is more than 30 and, despite being kind and sweet, She can't find man to start family with. But, She found candidate in caracter of soldiers Viktor.
Anna Pozdnyakova (Marina Neelova) is 30 years old and, despite the fact that she is kind and nice, she can not find herself a man with whom she could find a family happiness. She seemed to found a suitable candidate as a prominent military Victor (Alexander Fatushin), but he at first sight falls in love with Anna's friend Raisa (Natalya Andreichenko) and marries her. Then Anya makes out a holiday at her own expense and goes to the south, in Sochi. A lot of sad and funny adventures, she will experience before the lonely and restless Sanya (Leonid Kuravlyov) will make her an offer.
