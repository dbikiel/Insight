During a 48hr observation at a psych facility, Jack meets Wendy, a young woman with a unique obsession.
At Guildhouse Psychiatric Hospital, the resourceful inmate, Wendy, finds it hard to keep her feet on the ground, always trying to jump off a height. But, what is she trying to accomplish? Without a doubt, Wendy has concocted a masterful plan--and on Friday--the whole world will see that she was right all along. However, will they believe their eyes?
