In a typical British holiday camp during summer the employees are bored to hell. In order to enjoy the summer and have some holiday while working they celebrate erotic parties. This is great pleasure for them until the arrival of a new manager who tries to change the bad habits and turns their lives into hell.
Timmy Lea (Askwith) and his brother in law Sidney Nogget (Booth) run a holiday camp with complete disregard for the rest of the holidaymakers!. Picking girls for their liking life seems idyllic......that is until a new camp commandant takes over and starts whipping the staff into a new regime!. With an all star cast and following on from the previous 3 films, this 70's comedy will make you wish your next holiday was here!.
