Max sells trophies, but is much more interested in dancing than in selling. He is under his father's thumb, and his daughter is much more interested in Kurt than in learning to play the flute. To add insult to injury, the Argentinean tango championships are drawing near and Max still hasn't found a partner. In walks dancer Annie Brocca, and a whole range of new perspectives opens itself.
