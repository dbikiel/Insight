A man hires an ex-con to kill his sister's abusive husband, but the desperate act sets into motion a shocking series of events in this intense indie drama.
After an alcohol induced blacked-out night of drinking, Teddy (Fran Kranz) discovers he has severely beaten his wife, Molly (Jocelin Donahue). As he attempts to redeem himself to her she must decide whether or not she will take him back as her mother, Angela (Joelle Carter), continuously expresses her disapproval, all the while her brother, Gordon (Kenny Wormald), falls in way over his head as he hires an ex-con, Howard (Chris Mulkey), to kill Teddy.
