Martin is a successful writer whose wife suddenly disappeared. During a film shoot fifteen years later, Martin meets Angelique, who disappears the same night. The next day, police find her dead body and a mysterious investigation begins.
Martin (Sebastian Koch) is a talented screenwriter and novelist currently going through a difficult patch in his career. Yet after the death of the mysterious and alluring Angelique (Lotte Verbeek) and the arrival of her twin sister Therese, Martins world starts to change.
In this noir thriller nothing is quite what it seems.
