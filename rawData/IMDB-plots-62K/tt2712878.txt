After shockingly winning the Eurovision Song Competition in 2006, Mr. Lordi, the monster of Lapland, and his horror metal band lose almost everything. Will the man behind Mr. Lordi find a way to stay true to himself and make a comeback?
A heavy metal rocker living like an eternal teenager, Mr.Lordi, is in danger of losing his monster wonderland, six years after winning the world's biggest song contest.Can he rise once again and continue ruling his fantasy world, just like he has done since childhood?Or must he finally grow up?
