The last 7 hours of democratically elected former President of Chile Salvador Allende and his closest collaborators, inside the Palace of La Moneda, during the brutal military coup d'etat on Sept. 11, 1973, the day democracy in Chile ended. Based on true events.
"Allende" narrates the last hours of the former President Salvador Allende at the Government House, La Moneda. Anguish, despair, solidarity, love. Emotions that mix and overlap every minute that passes. The countdown to an outcome that will forever scar the history of Chile. The film shows Salvador Allende through a new perspective, unknown to most people. A portrait marked by the acuity in the narration of emotions and the richness of the details, reconstructed thanks to a precious testimony of those who accompanied him that day.
