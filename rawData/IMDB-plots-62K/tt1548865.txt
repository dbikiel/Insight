The abortion battle continues to rage in unexpected ways on one corner in an American city.
On an unassuming corner in Fort Pierce, Florida, it is easy to miss the insidious war that is raging. But on each side on 12th &amp; Delaware, people with opposing views stand locked in a heated battle. On one side of the street sits an abortion clinic. On the other side, a pro-life center often mistaken for the clinic it seeks to shut down. '12th &amp; Delaware' examines the daily battles of one of America's most intractable conflicts.
