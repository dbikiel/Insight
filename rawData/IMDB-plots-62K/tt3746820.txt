Eddie Izzard returns to stage in 2013 with the biggest stand-up comedy world tour ever &amp; his best live performance yet, Force Majeure Live. Filmed during his mammoth 2013/14 tour around 27 countries, Force Majeure Live takes you on a truly hilarious journey, offering a rare and often bizarre glimpse into the mind of the master of surrealism, Eddie Izzard, a man who has inspired several generations of comics and who is universally recognized by fans and comics alike, as one of the worlds greatest-ever stand-ups.
