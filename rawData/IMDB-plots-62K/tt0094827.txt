A journalist agrees to meet an obscene caller at a bar, and ends up witnessing a murder.
Anna receives a phone call in her home and she thinks wrongfully what is her boyfriend. The caller, an obscene, wakes her hidden passions and with each call is forming a bond of complicity and eroticism between them. A game of sexual confessions and dark fantasies that it turns dangerous when Anna is witness of a murder, and she will suspects what the mysterious caller maybe can be the killer.
