A security specialist (Nancy Bolan) is hired to deliver a valuable computer. She is joined by her former lover who has plans of his own for the computer.
As head of security for HTI corporation, Nancy Bolan must deliver a top-secret computer. She devises a brilliant plan utilizing two decoy computers, but a sadistic madman and his terrorist organization are somehow able to anticipate her every move. Nancy soon finds that she can trust no one as she battles her way through to a final, bitter showdown. Hell has no fury ...
