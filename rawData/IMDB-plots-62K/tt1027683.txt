Best friends fly home-built, high-tech planes to deliver drugs from Mexico to California in order to fund their Hollywood life-style.
Kill Speed is a high-octane, youth-oriented, Top Gun meets
Fast &amp; Furious tale about best friends who fly home-built,
high-tech planes to deliver Mexican-manufactured, crystal-meth
throughout rural California in order to fund their Hollywood
lifestyle until their group is infiltrated by a beautiful,
young woman who is not what she pretends to be.
