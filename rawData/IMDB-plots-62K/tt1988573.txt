Exposed by his girlfriend through a video on the Internet, Bruno tries to revive his reputation, but all he can do is get into a series of traps (Cilada, in Portuguese). Cilada.com is a comedy about love and betrayal that shows the power of the Internet to transform small intimacies and slips in fame and constraints globalized.
In Rio de Janeiro, advertising agent Bruno has sex with a woman during a wedding party. However, they are seen by the guests and his girlfriend Fernanda feels humiliated by him in front of their friends. Fernanda gets even with Bruno exposing a video in Youtube where Bruno has premature ejaculation after 10 seconds of sexual games with her. Bruno is mocked by his friends and the video becomes a sensation on the Internet. Bruno tries to reinvent himself having sex with other women to improve his reputation, but he does never succeed in his intentions and misses Fernanda.
