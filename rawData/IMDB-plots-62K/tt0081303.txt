The Creation of man is made by hatching from a egg. Eve is created from a sand that Adam makes to look like woman. Eve and Adam watch as animals mate. After Eve and Adam eat a forbidden apple they make love. In their adventure they face many obstacles, from a giant bird to cannibals. In the end Adam and Eve relize their love for one another and have a child. Paradiso Blu is also known as Blue Paradise and Adam and Eve Against the Cannibals.
