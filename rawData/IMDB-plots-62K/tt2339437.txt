An underage house spirit named Kuzya finds his way into a typical Soviet flat and turns the life of a little girl Natasha into a fairy tale.
The first animation film in order of appearance and the second animation film in the story of the brownie Kuzya. It tells about the forced moving of Kuzya from a demolished wooden house to a city apartment, where the family of the modern girl Natasha (7 years old) moved.
