When a magical butterfly kisses the kind princess, Kara, the crime-fighting hero, Super Sparkle, emerges. Now, along with her strong cousin, they must deal with the Baron, the kingdom's real enemy. Will the super-girls teach him a lesson?
One day, after being kissed by a magical butterfly, Kara soon discovers she has amazing super powers allowing her to transform into Super Sparkle, her secret, crime-fighting alter ego who flies around the kingdom ready to save the day! But it's not long before her jealous cousin catches the butterfly and also transforms - this time into Dark Sparkle, her nemesis.
All that Kara--the benevolent young princess of Windemere--wants is to make our world a better place for everyone, and before long, her noble dream will become a reality when a magical butterfly kisses her. Imbued with a new set of extraordinary powers, Kara is now Super Sparkle--the courageous crime-fighting superhero--who is on the lookout for those in need. Against the backdrop of a petty rivalry, Super Sparkle and her jealous cousin, Dark Sparkle, must set aside their differences to deal with the kingdom's real enemy: the duplicitous Baron Von Ravendale. The power of friendship and love is stronger than evil. Will the super-girls teach the Baron a lesson?
