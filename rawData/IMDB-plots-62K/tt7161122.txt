A semi-biopic of how India's first Tamil Hip-Hop band, Hip-hop Tamizha came to be and the struggles frontman Adhi faced in life, both in personal and professional frontiers.
The story revolves around a young college guy and his desire to become a best independent musician,while most of the film is shooted in the college still there are a lot of comedy scenes and romance.vivek's role in the film proved to be very vibrant,A decent film to watch with your family
