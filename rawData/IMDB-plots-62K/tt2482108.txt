Soviet animation film based on the Russian folk tale " Daughter and stepmother".
An evil stepmother sent an adopted daughter out of sight. She ordered to sit in the forest until she spun the whole cart of wool. Masha got into the dugout to the bear. She cleaned in the upper room, cooked dinner, fed a mouse. The master came in the evening. He liked how the girl handled the affairs. During the night they kept all the wool with the mouse, and in the morning they sent Masha home with rich gifts.
