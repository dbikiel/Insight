A Christmas Elf accidentally takes off in Santa's sleigh, crash lands in a small town, and loses the magic toy bag. Beethoven must rescue the Elf, recover the bag from greedy crooks, and return the sleigh to Santa in time to save Christmas.
When Santa's magic toy bag is stolen from the workshop, an elf escapes and lands in the hands of Beethoven the dog! As the countdown to the town's big parade continues, Beethoven and the Christmas elf must hunt down the thief and get the bag before its too late.
