A fantasy about the lonely boy who is transferred from his dull life with his adoptive parents to the land where his real father is the King. In that country, he sets out on a quest, together with his new friend, to destroy the evil Knight Kato.
A young boy named Bosse lives in misery with his cruel old aunt and uncle, until one night, he is whisked away to the magical land of Faraway. He finds that his real father is the King, and he is Prince Mio. Along with his friend Jum-Jum, he sets out to defeat the evil knight Kato, and free the children that Kato has enslaved.
