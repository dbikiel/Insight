Livingston is trying to merge the Russians, Chinese and Italian gangsters into one group with huge amounts of money and power. FBI agent Susan and DEA agent Max are trying to stop them.
FBI agent Diane Norwood is ready to do almost everything, even to spoil her own wedding, in order to bring down Livingstone, big money launderer. In course of her action she meets great obstacle in shape of Max Parish, agent from rival Drug Enforcement Agency who also wants Livingstone. After two of them reluctantly join forces, they track down Garret Lawson, one of Livingstone's hitmen.
