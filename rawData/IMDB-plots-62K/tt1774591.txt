Feeling confined by their small town and overbearing parents, Annie and Jules hatch a scheme of running away. But as the girls soon find out, consequences can blindside you. Sometimes violently.
TEENAGE COCKTAIL is a sensitive look at the tornado that is juvenile love. Annie and Jules, two high school girls, bored and without a sense of consequence, set out for excitement and devise a dangerous scheme to escape their small lives. A plan that quickly spirals out of control and tests their childlike belief of immortality.
