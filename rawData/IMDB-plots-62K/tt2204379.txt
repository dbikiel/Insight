Lost souls enter The Devil's Carnival, where they're each faced with the sins they committed during life.
Three individuals have accidentally stumbled Into a carnival...in hell, run by the old Scratch, themselves. They soon finds out that THEY are the 'entertainment', and moreover the 3 are doomed to repeat the very sins which delivered them to the carnival's doorsteps.
In this dark musical 3 Lost souls enter The Devil's Carnival and face its hellish entertainers and are faced with their sins.
