Clifford is a ten-year-old troublemaker, who puts not only his parents through hell, but also his uncle with whom his parents have discarded him to.
In this dark comedy, a mischievous ten-year-old boy named Clifford is sent to his Uncle Martin's for the weekend to get out of his father's hair. It turns out he has a dying obsession to go to Dinosaur World, a theme park near Martin's house and nothing will get in his way to get there.
Clifford is sent to visit his uncle Martin in Los Angeles while his parents attend a business trip. Clifford has an obsession to go to a theme park called Dinosaur World which Uncle Martin promises to take him to. However, after Uncle Martin is forced to renege on his promise due to his work obligations, Clifford resorts to destroying his Uncle's life in any means possible.
