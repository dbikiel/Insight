The first Venezuelan supernatural thriller: Dulce is a mother of two who suddenly experiences terrifying encounters with mysterious apparitions inside her old house, a place where a terrible prophecy is about to be unleashed. Thirty years later, an elderly Dulce returns home to decipher the mystery and the tragedy that have tormented her for so long.
A mother dedicated to her children experiences terrifying encounters in her house, and as she starts to lose the grip on her sanity, her marriage deteriorates, while the inexplicable and seemingly supernatural events in her surroundings become more intense and disturbing. Found guilty of her husband's death as well as her older son's strange disappearance, she's interned in a penitentiary, and released on house arrest thirty years later, when the odd, sad and sinister twists of her life begin to haunt her again and slowly reveal a dark truth about the very building she lives in
