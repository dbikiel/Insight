Bugs and Elmer supply new lyrics to Rossini's opera.
Behind the Hollywood Bowl stage which is playing the opera, The Barber of Seville, Bugs Bunny flees into the backstage area with Elmer Fudd in close pursuit. Seeing his opportunity to fight on his terms, Bugs raises the curtain on Elmer, trapping him on stage. As the orchestra begins playing, Bugs comes into play as the barber who is going to make sure that Elmer is going to get a grooming he will never forget.
In hot pursuit of Bugs Bunny, Elmer Fudd suddenly finds himself on stage at the Hollywood Bowl where The Barber of Seville is being staged. Bugs uses the situation to his advantage and has Elmer in the barber chair where he give him the full treatment.
