A writer moves into a mansion and things begin to go bump in the night.
Jebin (Ashok Selvan)is a budding writer waiting to get his work published. His long-time girlfriend Aarathy (Sanchita) is the only person who can handle his moods. Jebin is also upset that his father (Nasser) left him with nothing other than a villa in Puducherry. And when he reaches the villa, it unlocks some dark secrets.
