It's summer and Sune Anderssons family is planning a holiday in Greece. Sunes father, Rudolf is sulking because then he won't be able to go on his beloved fishing trip. But after seeing how much the Greek trip is going to cost, he comes up with a great idea: Let's go on a camping trip instead. A lot is happens during this trip and after a while Rudolf discovers that the camping trip might not be as cheat as he thought.
The middle child Sune and his family is suppose to go to Greece on their holiday, but after a minor mishap at the travel agency, Sune's dad Rudolf decides that a trip to Greece would be too expensive, so they bring out their old caravan instead. Rudolf has an accident and they all go to the hospital where Sune falls in love on first sight with a cute girl. He tries to make a good first impression, but fails miserably. He will have another chance though when his and the girl's families wind up at the same camping.
