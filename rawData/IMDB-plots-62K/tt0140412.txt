A new professor cleans up a crime-ridden college
Dr. Selvan (Kamal) is appointed as Vice Principal to Shaktivel college, where goons under college politician Ramesh (Karan) hold the campus hostage. Selvan, a former union leader and hoodlum himself, has ideas on how to clean up the act, and romance English lecturer Vasanthi (Gauthami) in the meantime.
