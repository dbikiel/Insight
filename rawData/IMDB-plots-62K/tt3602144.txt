An attractive young woman is robbed by her business partner in Macau. She meets and forms a connection with a handsome man, who proposes a plan: that she apply for work on a luxurious yacht to assist his boss, the difficult, old and infirm chairman of a large company, with the aim of manipulating him so that they can get their hands on his vast wealth.
