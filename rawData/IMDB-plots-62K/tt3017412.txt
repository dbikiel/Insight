A confused writer goes on the search of inspiration for his next story and falls in love with a best seller author who doesn't believe in love.
An erstwhile bestselling writer gets attracted to another writer who has replaced him at his publication. While his only chance to get back some of his fame (&amp; money) is to write a Hollywood meets Bollywood kind of script for a sleazy single screen hero who wants to make it big at the multiplex as well.
