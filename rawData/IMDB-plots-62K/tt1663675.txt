Eugene Stimpson is a superhero. Or, at least he plays one at birthday parties. His career hasn't helped much with the ladies, until he runs into an old school flame, Emily, who finds his career choice compelling. However, when Eugene's high school nemesis, Todd, gets a job playing a rival superhero, Eugene must keep his true identity hidden and finds himself competing for the affections of the same girl. Can he keep his identity concealed long enough to make his move and defeat Todd, or will the mounting lies be too much for him to handle?
