A stranded astronaut's race against time to get back home.
In a distant future and after a deep space war, an astronaut stand alone in an enormous starship, orbiting a planet meanwhile around him can be seen the consequences of the war, with all kind of space trash and rests of destroyed ships. Trying to reactivate his own ship and find some way to use its energy cells, the astronaut awaits for a signal of any ship traveling close to him in the hope that can be rescued. But with time running against him, there's not a moment to lose for backing to his home planet and meet again the woman he loves.
