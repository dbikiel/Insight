When the police discover that their motorcycles are concealing heroin, Waco (Robert Porter) and his motorcycle gang hides out in a desert convent. A highway patrolman (Billy 'Green' Bush) hunts down the gang after they kidnap a nun, Sister Anna (Tippy Walker) and flee the convent. Soon Waco and the young nun fall in love and she is forced to decide whether or not to leave the church for him.
They're a New Breed of Riders... On a new kind of trip... the high is heaven... the low is hell! once the young novice, uncertain in her commitment to God, tasted the lips of the man with the long hair and piercing blue eyes, she knew there was no way back. Her only chance for freedom was to betray him... to the sadistic border patrol. He wanted her... but how could he change her life? Their only crime... they wanted to be free!
