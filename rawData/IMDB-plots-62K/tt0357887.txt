The young Marisa, after having inherited from her aunt, gets involved in blackmail and crime.
Young Marisa, after having inherited from her aunt (who, moreover, was murdered) a pension located in the capital, finds herself, against her will, entangled in a squalid history of blackmail, mafia and crimes. She will emerge unscathed almost miraculously, thanks to Carlo's help, a handsome young man who, later will become her new man.
