Uncompromising police chief Durai Singam battles against a criminal operation dealing in deadly toxic waste.
Following the prequel, Duraisingam is transferred to Mangalore to investigate the death of the commissioner. In that process he gets involved with Australian toxic waste dealing mafia. Also Duraisingam discovers that Australian mafia is involved in this. The rest of the story is how Duraisingam catches the culprit though he face many work pressures and political pressures
