A little Gruffalo ignores her father's warnings and tiptoes out into the snow in search of the Big Bad Mouse.
Having been warned by her father not to go in search of the Big Bad Mouse, the Gruffalo's child defies him and sets out into the woods. After meeting the fox, owl, and snake, she discovers that the mouse is, in fact, little, and she determines to eat him. However, he is resourceful as ever, and has a plan to scare her off.
