Isobel Gray plans a storybook Christmas with her boyfriend, including a snow-covered cottage in the Northwest, and a carefully composed bucket list of classic holiday traditions. But when the boyfriend goes AWOL, the list proves challenging and a tempting new romance turns her life upside down. Will Isobel have a White Christmas ending under the mistletoe?
Isobel Gray is a young fashion designer who travels to Fall River, Oregon, "the perfect Christmas town" alone, when her fashion designer boyfriend Brett Bishop is suddenly called away on business. Isobel brings along her Christmas bucket list a collection of holiday traditions she's longed to experience since childhood. With Christmas approaching, Isobel meets Jamie Houghton, a handsome local contractor and volunteer fireman who is happy to assist Isobel in completing her list but also leaves Isobel asking if Brett is truly "the one."
