A documentary that examines the relationship between celebrity and society.
One night in Los Angeles, Adrian Grenier, star of the HBO series "Entourage," encountered 14-year-old paparazzo Austin Visschedyk. Astounded and inspired by the fast-talking, faster-snapping teen, Grenier decided to turn the cameras on Visschedyk in an effort to better understand this unique teenager&amp;#700;s world and gain insight into what motivates people to stalk the famous.
