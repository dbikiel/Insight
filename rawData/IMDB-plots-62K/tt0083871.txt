The misadventures of three soccer fans, all played by Abatantuono.
Three episodes. In the first Donato is the head of all the supporter groups of AC Milan (soccer). In the second Tirzan a lorry driver, exchanges his truck with another to follow his team (Juventus) to Belgium. In the last one Franco, an Inter FC fan, is convinced by his friends to be the winner of Totocalcio (Italian football bets).
