About The War between Sweden and Denmark, and the providence "Skåne" at late 1600Ac
A Swedish fictional tv series about a resistance movement in Skåne (south of Sweden) during the 17th century when they and the Danes were fighting against the Swedish rule in Skåne.The tv series was aired in 3 parts between 25-27 december 2006.For people wondering the people in the resistance movement are called "Snapphanar".This tv series has no subtitles but if you buy the dvd, it does have multi subtitles, even in English.However, dvd is inregion 2 and won't play in USA unless you have a "All region" dvd player.Plot information got mostly from YouTube and amazon.com sites.
