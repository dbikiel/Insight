A wealthy lawyer begins to suspect that his inattention to his wife has resulted in her taking on a lover. It turns out that he's right, but her lover also has a lover--and when HIS girlfriend disappears and he's charged with her murder, the lawyer's wife asks her husband to defend him.
Drama, romance and mystery plunge two socialites into a whirl of adventure as they tangle with murder in an attempt to save the life of an innocent man. Mark Latham, suave and sophisticated criminal attorney, battles for the love of his estranged wife, Erica, who unwittingly becomes involved with a murder suspect. The suspect, Tony Clark, finds himself not only falsely accused of murder but is to be defended by the husband of the woman he loves.
