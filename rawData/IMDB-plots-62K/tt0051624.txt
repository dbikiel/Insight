A GI on furlough attends a Folies-Bergères show with two of his mates (and his superior).He falls in love with a dancer ,Claudie,the star of the theater .Pretty soon,abetted by the piano player ,he becomes an artist /singer ,and part of the revue .He marries Claudie and now they are partners in life and in work .But a misunderstanding provokes jealousy and their marriage is on the rocks.
