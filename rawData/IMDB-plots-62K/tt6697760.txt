Hailey, an ex DA's prosecutor, uses her skills and connections when her friend Pam goes missing in the middle of selling her dead parents' big estate.
After Hailey Dean agrees to help Pam dispose of her parent's estate, her friend disappears without a word, Pam's boyfriend suffers a serious accident, and Hailey's boyfriend contracts a serious illness. Hailey must use skills she developed as a former prosecutor to determine if these events are connected.
