Finnish documentary about Finnish soccer player Jari Litmanen and his long football playing career. Jari tells openly about being an international sport icon, the European football teams he played for, like AFC Ajax, Liverpool FC and FC Barcelona, as well as his private life and the sacrifices he made due to a lot of injuries. Also a lot of well-known football colleagues comment on their time playing with Jari Litmanen: Puyol, Xavi, Bergkamp, Blind, Frank de Boer, Ibrahimovic, Gerrard, Van der Sar, a.o., as well as former coaches: Van Gaal, Houllier, a.o.
