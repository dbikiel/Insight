The creation of Bruce Springsteen and the E Street Band's 1980 double album "The River" is recalled in this new rock-doc that includes archival footage as well as recent interviews.
Incorporating previously unreleased footage from the tour, an intimate discussion with singer - songwriter Bruce Springsteen, photographs and unheard demos and outtakes, a condensed look is taken at the creation of his 1980 double album "The River."
