After he mends a marital rift between a vacationing young couple, the bored, fragile wife falls hopelessly in love with the husband's ex-colleague who is married to a long-suffering and emotionally and physically scarred woman. The couple soon run off to Greece together to pursue the romance.
Murray Logan (Peter Finch) and Sam Bonner (Arthur Hill) are old friends who work for the same company. Both men are in loveless marriages, but for different reasons, and while vacationing, the bored wife falls in love with her husband's friend. The pair jet off to Greece in their attempts at making this affair into a romance.
