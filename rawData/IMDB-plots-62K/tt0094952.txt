The true story of David Rothenberg whose disturbed father set him on fire.
David is a six-year-old boy who is caught in the middle of his two feuding (&amp; divorced) parents. His father, Charles, kidnaps him to California, where he eventually sets him on fire as a means of revenge against Marie, David's mother (Young David receives third-degree burns over 90% of his body). This movie chronicles David's struggle to recover and the obstacles he faces along the way.
