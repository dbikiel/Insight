A mechanic takes his family to a car race and a series of events occur which brings problems, betrayals, violence and the unexpected death of an elderly person.
Love, food, sex and death mix together in an explosive cocktail when a mechanic (Fábregas) takes his family (old granny Sara García included) to attend a car race. The innocent picnic becomes a feast, but then the old granny dies.
It is an interesting approach to the enmeshed Mexican family, usually consisting of three generations, with the double values where men can do anything and women must be faithfull. We can also see the paramount importance of the mother in Mexican culture portrayed by the famous actress of the Golden Yeras of mexican Films Sara García. It is a satire than can be seen as an exageration, performances are intense and faithfull to the interactions among Mexican males with some drinks....
