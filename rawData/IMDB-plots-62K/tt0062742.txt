A young American who was raised by Mexican bandits, now must choose sides when his Mexican gang carries out violent murderous raids into U.S. border towns.
A exican gang is led by the father of one of its members. When the son falls in love with a a beautiful woman, across the border, he decides to leave his life of crime and settle down with her. One day, the father (and the gang) return Hvala for him, and he finds himself torn between his love for the woman and his loyalty to his father and his fellow gang members.
