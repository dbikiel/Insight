A young mother living in the Irish countryside with her son suspects his increasingly disturbing behavior is linked to a mysterious sinkhole in the forest, and fears he may not be her son at all.
Trying to escape her broken past, Sarah O'Neill is building a new life on the fringes of a backwood rural town with her young son Chris. A terrifying encounter with a mysterious neighbour shatters her fragile security, throwing Sarah into a spiralling nightmare of paranoia and mistrust, as she tries to uncover if the disturbing changes in her little boy are connected to an ominous sinkhole buried deep in the forest that borders their home.
Single mother Sarah O'Neill and her young son, Chris, live in an isolated house near the woods outside a small town. In the woods: a giant sinkhole. In the town: a history of mothers convinced their children have been replaced by doppelgangers. After Chris wanders into the forest on his own, Sarah becomes one of them.
