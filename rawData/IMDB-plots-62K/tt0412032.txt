A single father is more occupied with his race horse than his daughter.
Fredrik, the single father of Magda, is too occupied with his race horse Kisa to see that his daughter has problems in school. But one day Magda brings home a new friend, Helena, caller at the local bingo hall. Fredrik is soon smitten by the frank and outspoken 29-year-old. Helena and a self-confident Magda help Fredrik to see why his horse never wins the races.
Fredrik struggles to become a successful trotter jockey, and at the same time, he is single and Magdas father. He has trouble finding his footing in life. Yet all that changes when he meets Helena, and begins to discover - step-by-step - what is most important in life. Finally, he realizes what it would take to win, and he decides to compete one more time.
