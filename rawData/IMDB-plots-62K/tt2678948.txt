A looming gangster after witnessing a bone chilling event, stands up against his father's prejudice.
Sivan (Mohanlal) is a well-respected don in the area of Madurai. After his driver was killed in an altercation with a few enemies, including a rowdy cop, he takes the driver's son Shakthi (Vijay) as his own. Shakthi grows up with a vengeful hatred towards the Police uniform and any other Khaki clothing. Even towards his eventual lover (Kajal), initially hating, but then seeing her good side, But it is when his Father desires him to wear the uniform, that turn the tables on the illegal work of Sivan. How Shakthi deals with his Father, and an unknown assailant forms the crux of the story.
A young Shakthi, loses his father in an attack on his employer, Shiva and his family. Shakthi manages to save Shiva's wife who takes him under his wings to raise him as his own son. Shakthi transforms into an invincible gangster under Shiva who commands a notorious gang in Madurai. The harmony in the gang is put to test when seeds from Shiva's crooked past come back to haunt him and he does the unthinkable. The ensuing tale depicts the aftermath and the unexpected turn of events lead to a gruesome climax.
Set against the backdrop of the historical city of Madurai, this action-oriented suspense thriller follows the twists and turns in the relationship between a father and his adopted son. Sivan (Mohanlal) is a don who adopts Sakthi (Vijay), his driver's son as his own after he is killed by a police officer. Sakthi grows up with an aversion to Khaki and hates cops. To save his father's crime syndicate, Sakthi is forced to join the police department, but soon tables are turned. How will the consequent conflicts affect the special bond between father and son?
