The rivalry between two brothers reaches a fever pitch during a charity swim competition.
A story of 2 brothers: Robbie - a local athlete and celebrity who happens to be blind and his brother Bill - who's helped Robbie through everything for years. Bill falls in love with a girl, Rose, but as luck would have it she starts dating his brother Robbie. Robbie has to decide whether to put his own emotions above his brother or to be the bigger man.
