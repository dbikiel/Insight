Each morning a group of people meet in the swimming pool. They aren't very close together really, but just know eachother from swimming. Loes, one of the swimmers, gets arrested though and the police accuse her of being member of a criminal organization of which all these morning swimmers are part. When interrogated, Loes begins to tell the story of what really happened.
