While conducting an investigation into the mysterious death of a fellow officer, a cop's own reputation is questioned when a suspect in his custody is shot.
The murder of a fellow cop is investigated by ace Inspector Sethupathi who approaches the case intelligently taking the untraveled route to arrive at a solution. The case with all its twists &amp; turns ultimately makes him go head-on with the big shot Vaathiyar, which puts his job and family at stake.
