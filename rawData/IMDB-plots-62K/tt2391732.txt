The film seeks to stress that only situations compel people to change and do things that are not in their nature.
Sasikumar, a son of rich landlord in Kandamanoor leads a happy life and spends all time with his friends. Many times, he goes out of the way to help his friends. One day, he goes to ensure that his friend's (Inigo Prabhakaran) love is conveyed to a girl (Lakshmi Menon), who happens to be his ex-flame. As it happens, she falls in loves with Sasikumar again. But trouble begins here. A murder happens and the blame falls on Sasikumar and then some twist and turns that forms the movie climax.
