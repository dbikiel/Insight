Three men unwittingly become a prey of a ruthless don after their quick-rich schemes goes awry.
Na Maloom Afraad is a story of three reckless poor struggling souls, running after their individual ambitions and desires, brought together by one incident which makes their not so simple life into a thrilling roller coaster ride of numerous ironic twist &amp; turns. The three characters run around in the chaotic city of Karachi bringing out the craziest plan to save their love, life and asses.
Troubled by circumstances, three dream-chasing men devise an ingenious plan to have a better life. Little did they know that they are in for a ride of their lives.Crossing paths with a number of colourful characters; from a tough-talking gangster to Russian mafia. Mayhem ensues!
