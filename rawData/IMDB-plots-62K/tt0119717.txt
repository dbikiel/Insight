Lester is an occasional substitute teacher and he's very jealous. He is jealous about the last boyfriend of Lester's slightly wacky current partner Ramona - arrogant best-selling author Dashiell. Lester joins Dashiell's therapy group under an alias to find out if Dashiell still has any feelings for her.
Would be-author Lester Grimm's insecurity is worst in relationships. When he finally has a steady girl, Ramona Ray, but stumbles on a book of her dashing former boyfriend Dashiell 'Dash' Frank and discovers where he's in therapy, pathologically jealous Lester joins Dr. Howard Poke's group, under his mate Vince's name. He thus befriends Dash, but while digging for what he doesn't really want to know strains his friendship and his relationship as things are further complicated.
