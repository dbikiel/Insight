A pair of cops investigating a drug invasion stumble upon a mysterious bank vault.
Waters and Stone are two nobody police officers who work in the evidence room of the Las Vegas Police Department. When Stone discovers an unusually high bail receipt in connection to a drug bust, the two friends set in motion a plan to find the source of the money.
Cage at his best, unveiling his true hairline, masterminds a scheme to try and score the articles within a mysterious vault he believes is bountiful as his holmesian deduction shines through while his inept social characteristics make this film an honest view of a man who understands his lot in life, but half hazzardly tries for more all the while he has been waiting, no, expecting for this moment to come: when fortune comes a knocking he answers the door
