In the dimly-lit and empty corridors of an ophthalmic hospital during the night shift, Emily, a young patient recovering from an operation with a gauze wrapped around her eyes, wakes up to the squeaky noise of a wheeled surgical cart inside her room. Petrified, the young girl calls for help, terrified by the palpable darkness and an ominous feeling that she is being watched. Could that be the tireless good nurse who has come to take the bandages off?
