Patty Vare falls off a horse and is found unconscious by preparatory school student John Baker. He takes her to his dormitory. As he quickly discovers, she is hiding from something. For John this becomes a road to maturity and for Patty, it's a way back to love and tenderness.
Patty Vare has a secret she is hiding. And after the police come to her door asking her questions, she tries to leave the area on her horse. But she falls off her horse and is knocked unconscious. She is later found by John Baker and two other boys who go to the local preparatory school. John and Patty develop a relationship, and in this relationship John finds a way to leave his verbally abusive father and the school that he hates.
