A successful, city-based construction manager finds herself reconsidering her cosmopolitan life choices when her employer relocates her to a folksy seaside town.
Successful Evi loves her busy life in Rotterdam. She lives in an apartment to die for with her boyfriend Len and works really hard as an engineer. She is called in to see her boss: she is being relocated. She can't wait to hear her dream come true. But then, to her complete dismay, she hears that she is being relocated not to Rio de Janeiro, but to - Terneuzen, a small town in the south of the Netherlands.
