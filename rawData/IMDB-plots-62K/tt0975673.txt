Expect the unexpected when George Lopez hits the stage in his first HBO solo stand-up special. Performed in front of a packed house at the Dodge Theater in Phoenix, Arizona, the show will be a no-holds-barred affair from the comedy star.
Superstar comedian/actor George Lopez, one of the premier
comedic talents in the entertainment industry, makes his
HBO solo debut performed live in front of a packed house
at the Dodge Theater in Phoenix, Arizona. Lopez delivers
a hilarious routine touching on his own Latino roots,
immigration and naturalization, modern-day kids,
old-school values, interracial relationships, the e-coli
outbreak, and the future.
