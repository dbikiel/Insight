Four best friends plot to rob a bank during their high school graduation ceremony in order to help out one of the teen's sick relatives.
Four best friends, about to graduate from high school, must find a way to raise money to help a family member in need. When one of them discovers her banker father having an affair, the foursome plots to rob his bank during graduation ceremonies. When things don't go according to plan, they end up learning more about themselves in one day than they ever did in school.
The film follows a group of high school seniors who are uncertain where life will take them after they get their diplomas. One is concerned about a family member in need of medical treatment; another is worried that her banker father is having an affair. The seniors hatch a plan to rob his bank during their upcoming graduation ceremony.
