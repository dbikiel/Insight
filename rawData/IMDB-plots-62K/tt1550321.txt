A cartoon based on the works of Nikolay Gogol. Handsome blacksmith Vakula who fell in love with village beauty, Oksana, is ready to do anything for her liking. Even to fly on the devil's back to Saint-Petersburg and ask the Empress for the slippers that he can present his sweetheart.
The action of the animated film takes place in Dikanka, in Ukraine. Nobody noticed, how two are spinning in the sky: a witch on a broomstick, which is collecting stars in her sleeve, and a devil who hides a month in his pocket. The devil thinks that the darkness will keep the wealthy Cossack Korniy Chub at home, invited to Osip Nikiforovich for a drink, and the blacksmith Vakula does not dare to come to Chubov's daughter Oksana.
