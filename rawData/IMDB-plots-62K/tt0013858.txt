A hapless amusement park attendant finds his run away balloon ride has left him in a strange predicament.
Buster and Phyllis endure a number of outdoor adventures trying to prove to each other their survival skills. The balloon which lands Buster in the wilderness proves useful later on as their canoe is about go over a waterfall.
Drifting rapidly aloft with the wind atop a plump hot-air balloon, Buster, the novice aerostat rider, leaves a macabre carnival fun house attraction, only to find himself marooned in a bear-infested forest, utterly unfit for survival. Amid a plethora of wildlife adversaries, Buster must summon up the courage to prevail; however, how will he survive in the unforgiving wilderness, let alone find love?
