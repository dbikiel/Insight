An unhappy wife orders a guy she meets over the Internet to kill her, but the two of them fall in love.
Sick of her life, housewife Nancy just wants it to be over and done with, but rather than kill herself, she hires a stranger from the Internet to do the job for her. But fate takes a strange turn when she meets her killer and the two fall in love. Of course, Nancy realizes that love and murder do not naturally go hand in hand.
Nancy is a masochist housewife and a physically and psychologically destroyed woman. She is ignored by her selfish husband Albert, who does not have sex with her or pay any attention to her problems, dedicating his leisure time to play a virtual golf game in a special room in their fancy house. Carol, her psychoanalyst unsuccessfully tries to help Nancy to resolve her emotional issues with therapy. Nancy contacts someone over the Internet to release her from her painful life, and schedules a meeting with him in Baltimore.
