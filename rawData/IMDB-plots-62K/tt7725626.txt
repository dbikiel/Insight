A village ruffian, who settles disputes in his native, takes on a big shot when he tries to sort out the rough patch in his marriage after several years.
Thooku Durai is a charismatic village chieftain who settles disputes in his native strikes fear in the heart of his enemies. Disturbed by his involvement into feuds, Niranjana, his wife takes their daughter, Swetha and leaves him which changes his life.
