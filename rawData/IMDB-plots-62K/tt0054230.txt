Tender romantic comedy about an aspiring musician who arrives in New York in search of fame &amp; fortune. He soon meets a taxi dancer, moves in with her, and before too long a romance develops.
A romantic comedy about a dancer who lives in New York and a would-be musician who arrives in town to make his fame &amp; fortune, and platonically share an apartment. It's not too long before sparks fly as they struggle to make it in the city's 'rat race'.
