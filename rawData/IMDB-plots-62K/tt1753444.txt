After a tragic car accident, a family gets away to a remote mountain cabin to recover and rebuild their lives. During their vacation, a handsome man befriends them only to get revenge and destroy what is left of their fragile lives.
Brian Warner takes his workaholic wife Hadley and their teenage brat daughter Skylar to a mountain lodge to mend their bond after a marital separation. The owner, Al Paunovic, seduces Hadley and stirs until Brian admits his one-nightstand, by Al is gruesomely obsessed with what seemed just a car accident she caused.
