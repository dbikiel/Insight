A young man gets infected and gradually starts turning into a zombie.
A young man, while out on a field trip, gets bitten by an ill looking person. When he comes back home he starts getting sick and a slow but steady degeneration begins in his body. He is turning into a zombie. He leaves his girlfriend and moves into his own place. In order to survive he needs to eat human flesh. Against his own consciense, he kills people and eats them in his living room. But things start getting worse as his degeneration progresses.
Mark is a young man who one day while partaking in a research field trip stumbled across a very sick woman and decided to help her but she bit him on the neck so he ultimately ran away. He subsequently becomes very sick and collapses. His girlfriend Sarah reports him missing for three weeks. He realizes he is becoming a zombie and decides to hide away from people. He then comes to the realization that he must eat human flesh in order to survive. He at first tries to fight the hunger but he can no longer fight it and much to his disgust and conscience he then resorts to killing and eating people. His life starts to go down hill as the degeneration only progresses.
