Ooga Booga follows an innocent African American medical student who is brutally murdered by a dirty cop, but his soul is magically transferred into an action figure named Ooga Booga. With only his tribal spear and old girlfriend to help he takes to the streets and trailer parks to find the men that ended his once bright future.
A clean-cut young African American man passes the test to become a doctor. At a convenience store to get a slushy for his girlfriend, the store is robbed and the clerk shot. Two racist white cops mistake the man for the shooter and kill him. Reincarnated in the body of a doll, Ooga Booga, the young man, his girlfriend, and his friend, a washed-up kids' TV star known as Hambo, seek revenge and justice.
