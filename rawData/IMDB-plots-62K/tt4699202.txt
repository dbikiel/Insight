Based on true event this story revolves around kidnapping of Preeto which family has come to know from midnight call. The tales revolves as to how they family will be able to track Preeto..
Story of a family is intertwined with the city of Gurgaon. There is a rebel, in the house of ambition with a gory past and greedy present. As he gets in big trouble, so do people around him. What will be the outcome of the chaos? What future has in store for this family?
