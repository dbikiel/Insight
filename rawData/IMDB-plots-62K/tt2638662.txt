Four American counsellors at a European summer camp must deal with the outbreak of a rage-inducing plague that starts in the animals.
4 young Americans travel to Europe to work as counsellors for a summer camp there. Before the kids arrive, a rage-inducing, demonic disease spread rapidly from animal to person, resulting in the counsellors desperately trying to escape the illness. The camp's in the wilderness, and with the phone service, not any cars , they're desperate too stay alive and get help.
When young Americans arrive to work as summer camp counselors In Spain's wilderness, they get more than they bargained for. Much more. A water-born fungal disease turns them into savage killers, and being in the wilderness they're unable to contact anyone. To make matters even worse, they're being watched.
