A young down on his luck resident of Hell (Sammy Davis, Jr.) is given a chance to redeem himself by signing up a down on his luck retail accountant to sell his soul to Lucifer.
A bumbling assistant to Satan (Sammy Davis, Jr.) hasn't gotten a soul for him for 1,400 years. He gets one last chance when he's assigned get the soul of a down and out retail department store accountant (Jack Klugman).
