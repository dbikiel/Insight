Cassie moves to New York and jumps from one job to another until she lands a modeling gig.
Cassie has come to New York and goes to work as a model where her friend Gladys works. She falls in love with wealthy young Jerry who is already married. Gladys has the same probelm with her man Phelps.
Three girls from Hicksville decide to sample life in New York City with varying degrees of success. Gladys Kane (Mae Clark) is a kept woman in a Park Avenue apartment, Cassie Barnes (Jean Harlow) becomes a model in a couturier store and Dot (Marie Prevost) addresses envelopes in a business office. One discovers her man is already married and returns to Hicksville,another one commits suicide when her married lover tires of their romance and one happily marries a lovable chauffeur.
