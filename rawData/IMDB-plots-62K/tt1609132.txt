A famous actress finds herself captured by a morbidly deranged paintress.
When disformed artist Jeanne (Tinneke Caels), driven by jealousy, kidnaps the beautiful and
celebrated film actress Isabelle (Halina Reijn), a psychological thriller between life and death
emerges between the two women. Jeanne wants Isabella to starve so she can make a painting of
mankind's detoriation. Based upon the novel by Tessa de Loo
