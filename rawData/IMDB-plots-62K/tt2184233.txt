LIFE AT THESE SPEEDS is an uplifting and emotional romantic drama about the power of our happiest memories to sustain us in the worst of times. After a teenager's best friends die in a tragic accident, he finds that running allows him to remember them perfectly. Running, however, also brings him a certain notoriety, and he soon finds himself caught between keeping the past alive and making new memories in the present.
In one terrible incident, high school senior Kevin Schuler loses his girlfriend, his best friend, his coach and his track team. Without enough left for his small school to maintain him, he's sent to a different school as well. Recognized for his exceptional running ability, several can't wait to capitalize on the reflected glory of having him, oblivious that he's a kid lost in grief. Fortunately, his new coach wonders more about what's going on inside his head that makes him hell-bent for running.
