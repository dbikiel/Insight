A ventriloquist struggles to survive with no audience while he becomes lonely- his only friend his puppet.
Frank is a ventriloquist who has been at the same corner for the past 3 years. His puppet, Mr. Higgins, basically is Frank's more outgoing, more fierce personality. Frank does have a crush on Stephanie, his coffee provider, but it turns out that she doesn't like him back. Frank continues to get gloomy and receives a black eye thanks to Mr. Higgins. He returns home,and wakes up the next morning to find Mr. Higgins missing. Whilst looking for him, Frank encounters a woman.
