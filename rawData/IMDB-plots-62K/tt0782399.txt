Harold is a seemingly peaceful neighbor in a quiet midwestern neighborhood, but underneath, he's a murderous psychopath who sets his eyes on a couple that have moved in across the street.
Harold seems like the neighbor anyone would like to have, he is quiet and keeps to himself: Unfortunately he is a serial murderer and a psychopath who sets his sights on a young married couple who moves across the street from him. Moreover, the young married couple are not who they seem to be either.
Family tells the story of a young married couple that moves into a new home in a new city and finds out that their neighbor is not what he seems.
