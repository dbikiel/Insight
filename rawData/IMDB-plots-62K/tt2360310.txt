Igor, a plumber and Flora are a dysfunctional couple. They have many problems since they cannot be parents, Pina, tea woman is a single mother with a difficult child and wants to feel being desired by a man. When both know each other in the catacombs of the building where they work and the sparks jump off, sexual tension bursts between them, although they know that their behavior could harm third parties. Everything will be complicated when she decides to make a lion costume for her young son and he buys a photocopier for his wife. Spring will free them, filling their lives with passion and guilt.
