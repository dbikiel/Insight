A red balloon with a life of its own follows a little boy around the streets of Paris.
A boy makes friends with a seemingly sentient balloon, and it begins to follow him. It follows the boy to school, to the bus, and to church. Boy and balloon play together in the streets of Paris and try to elude a gang of boys that wants to destroy the balloon.
On his way to school, a young adolescent boy sees a red balloon tied to a lamppost, the balloon which he unties and takes with him. He treats the balloon with care, much like he would another human being. In return, the balloon becomes his friend in he no longer even needing to hold onto its string as the balloon willingly follows him around. But as he was attracted to the balloon in friendship, other children also become interested in it and try to take it away from him, not all with the best of intentions. The boy's efforts to protect his friend leads to his kindness being paid several times over in return.
