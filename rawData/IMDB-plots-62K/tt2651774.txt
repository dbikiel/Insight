Peter is the most ordinary person. He lives with his controlling mother and spends his days at the office, where he is the butt of his colleagues' jokes. All goes well till his sexually aggressive female boss decides to take him to Africa. And then his pretty colleague from the next office starts flirting with him, as well. Tired of all this female attention, Peter and his buddies sneak into an army base to pick psychedelic mushrooms. So begins the trip that will change Peter's whole life.
