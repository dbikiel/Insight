In the face of death you can really sense life. There are people who share their lives with the dead and some literally with corpses. A trip from Argentina to Manila, Cairo and Sulawesi.
NICE PLACES TO DIE accompanies people who share their lives with the dead and some literally with corpses. What are these people like who live on cemeteries or who keep a deceased at home? How is someones life affected when you deal with the dead every day? Told by the son of an undertaker the audience is taken on a journey from the stunning mountains of Argentina, the vibrant Manila and the earthy hard Cairo to the exotic island of Sulawesi to places that all have something in common. Life!
