The year is 2077, in this post-apocalyptic Western, two things are rare, guns and women. Nora and her friend and lover Daphine must struggle to survive. In a post government world where there are no laws or weapons, Nora and her best friend / lover Daph live an idyllic life in a sparse and rugged landscape. After a stranger arrives on their land Daph's curiosity takes her away to a violent and misogynistic psychedelic drug cult. Nora doesn't just want Daph back, she also wants revenge.
