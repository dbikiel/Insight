In a secluded mansion, the Hilton patriarch curses his family on the hour of his death. Soon, many of the heirs are murdered by a strange killer under the instructions of a witch who knows the secret of the Hiltons.
A rich, cynical wine-grower is cursing his family in the hour of his death, in particular his children whom he loathed for their material greed. Shortly after his burial, one by one, his relatives, are dying of unnatural causes. Soon, the police is investigating the dead man's sister who is said to be a modern witch with supernatural powers.
