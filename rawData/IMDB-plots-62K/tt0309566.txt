A gardener tries his best to make his salad plants grow. It is only when he cries that his tears finally water the field and the salads grow huge. The incredible size attracts a multitude of snails that quickly become giant too, causing disasters and panic in the nearby city.
In a distant planetoid, part of a multi-dimensional parallel universe where its hospitable planets balance freely atop a cosmic meadow, an industrious but hapless old farmer strives to make his vegetables flourish, however, to no avail. But when the weary cultivator's hot and silent tears of despair finally give the solution to this problem, before long, a completely different set of unforeseen hardships will emerge.
