Jolly LLB is an upcoming Bollywood comedy drama film directed by Subhash Kapoor. The film features Arshad Warsi, Amrita Rao and Boman Irani in lead roles. The music is composed by Krsna of Tanu Weds Manu fame. The film is expected to release on 15th March 2013.The film is a social satire on the law system of the country India. The story follows a struggling lawyer named Jolly (Arshad Warsi) who wants to make it big in life like his idol (Boman Irani). But when he is signed a court case against his own idol, Jolly must prove his worth and his skills as a lawyer and win the case! Jolly hails from a small town in India. To make it big as a lawyer and prove his worth, he moves to the city.
