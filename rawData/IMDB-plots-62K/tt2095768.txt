An unfiltered look in to the lives of 3 characters surviving amongst the most recent cycle of conflict in the Democratic Republic of Congo, otherwise known as the M23 rebellion.
THIS IS CONGO looks into the repetitive cycles of conflict in the Democratic Republic of Congo through a national army Colonel rising in the ranks and the latest cycle of war in the region. With questionable elections approaching in the fall of 2016, the cycle of war is likely to start again.
