After husband and wife aid workers, Dr. Brinks and Dr. Brinks, die in a plane crash, their grown children are reunited for the first time in years. It takes days for Marcus and Michelle Brinks to sort through the incomprehensible paperwork of their globe-trotting parents' estate. That's just enough time for Michelle to stir up her own trouble - The resulting chaos is a fine distraction for the siblings who refuse to face the grief of losing parents they hardly knew.
