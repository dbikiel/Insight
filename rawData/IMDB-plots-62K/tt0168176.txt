A mixed group of Czech tourists takes a fun-filled coach tour of sunny Italy.
The employees of an agricultural cooperative from Italy invite JZD Hostic employees to visit planning to visit Hostice, Czech village in turn. So the local peasant team organizes a tour to Italy. In addition to the sights, the a excursion of modern Italian cows was also the aim of the trip. After returning home, Hostice and its residents are waiting for a decision - what can be done for their guests? In the name of a lucrative lucky co-operation, a nudist beach will be created in Hostice.
