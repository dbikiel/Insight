In a Georgia swamp, a young hunter stumbles upon a wanted fugitive and his daughter, and helps them return to civilization.
A young girl and her father, who was unjustly accused of murder, seek refuge in a Georgia swamp until they are befriended by a trapper who penetrates the swamp in search of his dog.
The year is 1910 and a hunter, Ben Tyler, searching for his dog, strays deep into the treacherous Okefenokee swamp in Georgia. He comes across Jim Harper, who, several years ago was unjustly accused of a murder, is living and hiding in the swamp with his beautiful daughter, Laurie. Ben and Laurie fall in love, and Ben works on getting her father to come out of the wilderness and clear his name.
