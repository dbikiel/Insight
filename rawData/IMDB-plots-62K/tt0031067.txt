An unemployed woman discovers an abandoned baby on the steps of an orphanage, and accepts an offer to take responsibility for the child in return for a job.
Polly Parrish, a clerk at Merlin's Department Store, is mistakenly presumed to be the mother of a foundling. Outraged at Polly's unmotherly conduct, David Merlin becomes determined to keep the single woman and "her" baby together.
Unemployed Polly Parrish impulsively picks up a baby foundling, her playboy ex-boyfriend assuming its hers then offers her a job if she lives up to her motherly duties, the unknowing grandfather then steps in to find where his 'grandson' came from.
