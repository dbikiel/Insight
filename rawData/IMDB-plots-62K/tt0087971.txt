A man is forced to completely change his habits when he decides to move from the country to the city.
Artemio is a farmer and he lives in a house with his mother, in the country. Maria Rosa lives in the village nearby and she is the only girl he ever saw. Unfortunately she very ugly and she wants to marry him. Artemio decides to leave everything and to go to Milan looking for something better. In the city he meets Angela and falls for her. But Milan and Angela are too much for a simple farmer.
