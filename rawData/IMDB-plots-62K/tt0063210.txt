During an ocean dive, Miami gumshoe Tony Rome finds a woman's body with its feet encased in a concrete block and sets-out to solve the murder case.
Tony Rome is a Miami based detective who while diving in the ocean finds the body of a young woman. He is hired by Gronsky to find her killer. Tony has to sift through a stack of suspects, plus trying to elude the police.
Having come across a blonde with her feet encased in cement while on a diving trip, Miami Private Eye Tony Rome is helping out his police buddy on the case when he is hired by a very large guy to find a girl who just might be the same one. Rome is soon wanted by both hoods and cops, but help may (or may not) be at hand from the striking Kit.
