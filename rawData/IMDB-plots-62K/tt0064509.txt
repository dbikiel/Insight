After a successful robbery, the gang leader Jeff disappears with the loot, leaving his accomplices with nothing. Gang members decide to go and pull any information by any means about possible location of their ex-boss and their money from Eva, Jeff's girlfriend. One of the gang members, Laurent, Jeff's right hand, refuses to join and revolts against others. He is left under the hostile guard of one of the gangsters. Laurent fights his way out and rushes to see Eva. Laurent and Eva set out on a trip to find Jeff and find out the truth. Meanwhile other gang members are not going to give in as well..
