Despite a returning Marine's mundane reality, his haunting wartime past shepherds him into a magical and destructive new existence to expose his ultimate truth.
When Spencer Koll, a United States Marine, returns home from a horrific wartime experience, his mundane reality and the horrors of war lead him to gravitate towards a new and mysterious woman on her own determined journey. Together, they enter into a magical but destructive new relationship which ultimately exposes their essential truths.
