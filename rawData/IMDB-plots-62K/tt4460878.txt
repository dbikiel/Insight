An anthology series which centers on different characters being targeted by crazed serial killer Mick Taylor in the Australian outback.
When Eve and her family go on holidays to Australia and camp in the outback, Mick Taylor shows up and starts up a friendly conversation with the family but than he does something horrendous and unthinkable, though Eve escapes and starts illegally staying in Australia while she begins a journey to hunt down Mick Taylor to bring him to justice.
