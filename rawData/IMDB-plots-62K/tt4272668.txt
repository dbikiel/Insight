Man Ponka bet with rich Americans that he will not wash for 15 years and wins.
Rich American Sam, along with his younger cowboy brothers, John and Pete, comes to St. Petersburg to show himself to the country. In the market, he meets a trading guy Pronka Greznoy, from whom he buys bast shoes. Interested in Pronka, Sam offers him a bet: he doesn't wash for 15 years and trades, and John and Pete must follow him. Pronka agrees and becomes a super successful trader, literary character and movie hero.
