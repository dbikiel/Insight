This cartoon is not a literal presentation of the story of Icarus, but a parable of what a man may and may not do.
In ancient society, life flows calmly and slowly. Each has already found its place in life. People devote leisure to philosophical conversations - about the meaning of being and so on. But among them lives the eccentric - Icarus, who is trying to realize the insane, from the point of view of the progressive ancient society, the idea: to learn to fly like birds.
