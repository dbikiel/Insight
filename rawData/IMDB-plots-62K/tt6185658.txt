Join Barbie as she enters three different video games! Will she be able to save the lands?
Get ready to power up! When BarbieTM magically gets pulled into her favorite video game, she is excited to see she's transformed into a fun roller-skating character. In the game, she meets Cutie, the lovable cloud-shaped friend, and Bella, the roller-skating princess. Together, they soon discover a mischievous emoji is trying to take control of the game. As they travel from level to level, BarbieTM must rely on her amazing gaming skills and out-of-the box thinking to save her team and beat the game!
While inspecting her latest video game for bugs and glitches, unexpectedly, Barbie finds herself magically transported into the game she is developing. Baffled, yet amazed inside its colourful world, Barbie soon encounters Cutie--the happy tutorial cloud--which informs her that her creation is on the brink of disaster, as a malicious and rapidly spreading virus is infecting the game emojis. Now, with the help of some new friends--the beautiful roller-skating princess, Bella--and a good dose of teamwork, Barbie must progress through, and defeat every single frantic level of the game to prevent the uncontrollable virus from taking over. However, will Barbie manage to do it in time?
