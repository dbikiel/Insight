Two strangers feel a supernatural connection after being involved in the same accident.
I REMEMBER YOU tells the story of a chance accident that leads to an unexpected romance, which echoes across time. Leah and Samuel meet as strangers when Leah saves Samuel from almost drowning in the ocean. Immediately, both cannot shake the feeling that they have met before and that their chance encounter was not a coincidence. Gradually, they realize that they share an incredible past that now beginning to remember
