A complicated love triangle develops, which leads to sex, drugs, alcohol and romance.
Inspired by true events, Todd Verow's Tumbledown is an explosive cocktail- an emotional roller coaster ride through the dark sides of sexuality. A complicated love triangle develops after hunky Jay meets bartender Rick and invites him to spend the weekend with him and his partner in their country cabin. Soon, copious amounts of sex, drugs and alcohol lead to a dark obsession and even darker complications.
