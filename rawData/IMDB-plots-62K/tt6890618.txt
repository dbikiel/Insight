Get caught up in the tale of a new stock broker who dreams of becoming rich. Those dreams make him susceptible to a stock market scam that threatens to get him in trouble.
A rookie stockbroker Il-Hyun (RYU Jun-Yeol) dreams of making big money, but being a rookie without the right connections makes it difficult for got to survive in the cut-throat world of stock exchange market. So when he is approached by the Ticket (YOO Ji-Tae), an anonymous mastermind who secretly controls the stock market, he takes the chance despite the dire consequences.
