A man who works in a pharmacy as a part time job to aid his financial status finds out that his father has been exterminated by a savage brute and sought to avenge him, then unlocks a whole melancholic world of crime!
Taha is a medical representative in a reputable pharmaceutical company, who leads a normal life with his chair bound father, who finds out about a mysterious murder with no traces. After his own father is murdered by a bully, Taha discovers his aged fathers' notebook that would unleash a chain of even more mysterious killings.
